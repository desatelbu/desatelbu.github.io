Follow our journey through the wild rivers of Terrace BC, the lacs and rivers of Eeyou Istchee Baie-James and the magnificent Matapedia river in Gaspesie. We get out there in search of Big Pike, Atlantic Salmon, Steelhead and Coho Salmon. Follow us on this expedition in some of the most beautiful places on earth!
