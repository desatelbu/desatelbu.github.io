The Gap is a window into different lives and different generations.

Two people, from different age groups, discuss a shared experience that impacted their lives, how it affected them and how they dealt with it to ultimately emerge stronger.

In this episode Jo, 55, and Alice, 24, came in to our studio to discuss their experiences working as firefighters.

They talk about how they broke in to the fire service, the most dangerous things they did and their view on the Grenfell Fire.

With thanks to Josephine Reynolds and Alice Williams ( @alicerwilliams on twitter and instagram)
for taking part.
