He was born in the African country of Zimbabwe in 1961. He attended primary school in Zambia.

2. Spread your message
He returned to his native Zimbabwe in 1984 after a 17-year absence.

3. Take it one step at the time
He quit his job and set up his own company with the equivalent of US$75.

4. Form trustworthy partnerships

5. Use technology for good
The emergence of mobile cellular telephony led him to diversify into telecoms.

6. You have to earn trust
In March 2000, Masiyiwa left Zimbabwe, never to return to the country, and moved first to South Africa.

7. Empower people

He has used his wealth to provide scholarships to over 100,000 young Africans over the past 20 years.
