You are not giving yourself enough credit.

You are so focused on where you are not you can’t even see where you are; how much you have grown, and how far you’ve come.

If you want to feel more motivated, more inspired, and more empowered, focus on everything that’s going right. When you can celebrate all of the little wins that are happening each and every day, each and every week, you’ll realize you are not far away from what you want. You are very much on your way to making what you want a reality.
