Today the Krew are changing their age and whoever can transform best with the age they choose wins money. To choose their age the Krew has to spin the wheel to determine what age they have to transform to. Cilla had to transform into a 21-year-old, Maddy into a five-year-old, Anthony into an 80-year-old, and Liam into a year old. Once they arrived at Target mom put a little twist into this challenge, they had to spin the wheel on the phone for a budget. Anthony won the lowest budget, which was $10. What can he buy with $10? Anthony was lucky because he found a sales rack and pretty much found everything, he needed on it. He even found gray hair spray at the Halloween sales rack, and it was the last one. Anthony was very lucky to have found all that for only $10. Everyone else had a bigger budget, which made finding something wear much easier. Who do you think won this challenge? Who transformed into the age they choose? You must watch and see until the end to find out. We hope you enjoy this video as much as we enjoyed filming it.
