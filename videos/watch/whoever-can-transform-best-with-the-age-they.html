Today the Krew are changing their age and whoever can transform best with the age they choose wins money. Once they arrived at Target mom put a little twist into this challenge, they had to spin the wheel on the phone for a budget. Anthony won the lowest budget, which was $10. What can he buy with $10? Anthony was lucky because he found a sales rack and pretty much found everything, he needed on it. He even found gray hair spray at the Halloween sales rack, and it was the last one. Anthony was very lucky to have found all that for only $10. Everyone else had a bigger budget, which made finding something wear much easier. Who do you think won this challenge?
