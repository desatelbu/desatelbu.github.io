Anthony was lucky because he found a sales rack and pretty much found everything, he needed on it. He even found gray hair spray at the Halloween sales rack, and it was the last one. Anthony was very lucky to have found all that for only $10. Everyone else had a bigger budget, which made finding something wear much easier. Who do you think won this challenge?
