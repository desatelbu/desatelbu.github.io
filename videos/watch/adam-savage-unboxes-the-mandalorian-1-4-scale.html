Adam unboxes the new quarter-scale statue of The Mandalorian and Grogu from Sideshow! There’s a lot to appreciate about this imposing figure, from the marvelous sculpt of Mando in his weathered beskar armor to the engineering of Grogu’s floating pram help up by an armature hidden in the sweeping cape. It’s going to be a standout piece in Adam’s collection of Mandalorian figures in the cave!

The Mandalorian and Grogu Premium Format Figure: https://www.sideshow.com/collectibles/star-wars-the-mandalorian-sideshow-collectibles-300786
