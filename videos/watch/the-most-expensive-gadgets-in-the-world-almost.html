Almost everyone nowadays owns a smartphone, a laptop, a television, etc. Our homes are filled with tech gadgets that keep us entertained, and make our lives easier. But have you ever wondered if your favorite gadgets could be… more eccentric? Some companies have heard those concerns, and created some of the most extravagant, over the top gadgets you can imagine. You won’t have a hard time finding the world’s most expensive gems and metals in these products. Gold coatings and flawless diamonds are a standard in these gadgets that cost upwards of millions of dollars. Almost all of these gadgets focus on looks, and the materials that go into making them. Of course, the device itself is still fully functional, but when you have a giant pink diamond embedded in it, the gadget becomes secondary.

Some of the gadgets that have gotten the luxury treatment are more extravagant than others. Some go the straightforward route of loading the gadget with gold and diamonds, while some take the extra step to give their device a unique distinction. Alligator skin is a recurring inclusion in some of the items. And sometimes a company goes above and beyond to deliver a one of a kind, historic device. There’s one gadget that includes shavings of a T-Rex bone! If you owned that, you would always have the most interesting fact at the table.

Which of these gadgets has the most flair? Let’s find out and see how much you’ll have to save to buy one!
