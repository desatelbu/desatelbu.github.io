To kick off proceedings, this issue, we’ll be taking an in-depth look at the Slide Turn transition in kitesurfing. The Slide Turn comes in many shapes and forms, long ones, sharp ones, carvey ones, spray ones, an endless list of possibilities with the same dependable opportunity to change direction. In short, it never gets old and can only get better.
