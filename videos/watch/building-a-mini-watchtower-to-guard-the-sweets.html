Next, we assemble a special paper construction. So to speak, the prototype of our tower. Then comes the time of the most interesting part! We’re putting mini bricks on the watchtower. Our watchtower looks more like a real one now! But in order to finish our craft, let’s add some decor. Everything is ready!
