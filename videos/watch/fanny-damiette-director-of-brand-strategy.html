Fanny Damiette is the Director of Brand & Marketing Strategy for SSENSE, a Montreal-based fashion platform currently serving 114 countries and generating an average of 32 million monthly page views. In her role, Fanny leads various branding, marketing and customer insights initiatives for the company.
Prior to joining SSENSE, Fanny was a consultant and the head of trend forecasting at Saguez & Partners, where she worked on branding projects for leading commercial real estate and investment firms in Europe.
