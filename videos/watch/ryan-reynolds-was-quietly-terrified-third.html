Ryan Reynolds who stars alongside Dwayne ‘The Rock’ Johnson and Gal Gadot in “Red Notice” share with his costars more than just the screen. They all are proud parents to three girls and bonded over that while filming the upcoming Netflix film. Ryan revealed to Access Hollywood’s Sibley Scoles that he and wife Blake Lively didn’t find out ahead of time that their third child would be a girl, “When we had our youngest, I was quietly terrified that it was going to be a boy because I didn’t know, we didn’t know,” he said. Adding, “I didn’t know anything but girls.” “Red Notice” is out in select theaters now and on Netflix Nov. 12.
