Money talks, so detectives hope that an increase in reward money – $6,000 – will help lead them to whoever killed Leonard Wimberly, Jr. four years ago. Investigators say Wimberly was riding his three-wheeled bike on Masterpiece Road in Polk County around midnight on Friday, October 28, 2017, when a truck approached him from behind, ran him down, and took off.

Wimberly was on his way from his to his sister’s house, less than half a mile away. He was hit
