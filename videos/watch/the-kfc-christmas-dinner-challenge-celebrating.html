Celebrating the spirit of the season by taking on an enormous home challenge from KFC! In Japan, a bucket of KFC’s fried chicken is a Christmas dinner tradition - so I decided to bring that tradition back to the states and turn it into a Christmas food challenge! I gave myself just one hour to finish an 8 piece Original Recipe Chicken bucket, 1 Family Sized Cole Slaw, 1 Family Sized Mac N Cheese, 1 Family Sized Potatoes w/ Gravy, and 4 biscuits!!!

Hope ya’ll enjoy this festive challenge!
