Celebrating the spirit of the season by taking on an enormous home challenge from KFC! I gave myself just one hour to finish an 8 piece Original Recipe Chicken bucket, 1 Family Sized Cole Slaw, 1 Family Sized Mac N Cheese, 1 Family Sized Potatoes w/ Gravy, and 4 biscuits!!!
