In this ZEISS Optometric Digital Summit 2018 highlight, David E. Lederer, MD helps us interpret macular OCTs.

Optical coherence tomography has become a standard diagnostic tool for patients with macular pathology.

It remains important to understand not only the nomenclature to describe disease but also the implications associated with those descriptions.

This course is designed to help you navigate these challenges and get you interpreting macular OCT scans like a pro!
