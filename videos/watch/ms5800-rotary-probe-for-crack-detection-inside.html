Therefore, NDT inspectors need special probes (MRPC, +Point MRPC, SPNR) or bobbin-type multi-array probes. However, since these probes and rotating accessories are very expensive, the initial investment and maintenance costs are very high, which limits the application of heat exchanger tube inspection of various tube sizes in petrochemical plants.
