Average Net Worth By Age (Not What You’d Think)

Most of us don’t have an eye-popping net worth like the famous investor Warren Buffett or the CEO of Tesla, Elon Musk. Just because you’re not that wealthy doesn’t mean it’s pointless to know your personal net worth. In fact, it can be a pretty helpful tool that many people fail to utilize. Knowing how much money you have is beneficial because you can keep track of your progress and see improvements over time.
