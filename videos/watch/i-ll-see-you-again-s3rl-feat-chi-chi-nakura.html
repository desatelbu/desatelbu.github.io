From Nakura: "I can’t believe I had forgotten about this S3RL song, the original is so good. I was browsing S3RL’s remix packs on his website and seeing it available jogged my memory. I gave it the 2-step beat spin that I have been liking lately, I figured it would work with the melody and I was right. I really like the sound I was able to give to the song and I want to try remixing more S3RL stuff in the near future."
