Remastered in HD to celebrate the 30th Anniversary of Better The Devil You Know.

One of Kylie Minogue’s signature songs, “Better The Devil You Know” was the lead-off single from her third LP “Rhythm Of Love”.

The track is a staple of Kylie’s live sets and was a UK number two hit in May 1990. The track also featured on the soundtrack to the Richard Grieco film “If Looks Could Kill”.
