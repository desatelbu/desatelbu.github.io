The world of STEM is a BIG wide world of opportunity, waiting to be discovered!
Tom Warrender is the most sought after inspirational STEM & Careers speaker in UK schools.

He is famous for putting himself through real full blown medical procedures in hospitals for fun & then repeating them live on stage to inspire the next generation of health professionals.

The most memorable procedure he has been through is a bronchoscopy, where he had a camera put up his nose and into his lungs!

He creates aspiration raising posters, videos, books & workshops that help teachers support their students, showing them it is not all about getting top grades and there is an amazing world of careers that are achievable with levels 9-4 at GCSE!"
