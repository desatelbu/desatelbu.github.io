A 39-year-old amateur rapper is in custody after allegedly driving into spectators at a holiday parade in Waukesha, Wisconsin, on Sunday, killing five people and injuring at least 48 others. Documents obtained by Inside Edition show Brooks has a history of allegedly using his SUV as a weapon.
