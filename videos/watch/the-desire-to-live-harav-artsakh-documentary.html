Today she says: "May my people live in peace! We may have to cut one bread in half, we may face difficulties, but we wish to live on our native land and be independent."
A local resident of Harav talks to us about what peace means for him, how he only wishes to live in Artsakh. He recalls the times when his best friend was an Azeri boy.
