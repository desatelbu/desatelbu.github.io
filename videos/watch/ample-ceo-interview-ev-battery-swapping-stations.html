Once discharged battery modules are removed from the car, they are placed on shelves so that they can be charged and ready for the next vehicle.

An Ample EV battery is made out of lego-like battery modules that can accommodate any make, design, model or driving profile ‚Äî from commuting to ridesharing to last-mile delivery, even autonomous.
