Forced wipe day raiding is the fastest path to riches in Rust - and on this forced wipe, we did exactly that. After acquiring a ludicrous quantity of raw materials and components, we built up our main base and crafted as many guns and as much gear as we possibly could before contesting the major objectives around the map. It didn’t take long for us to establish a feud with another competitive group with a similar gameplan.


Provocation is batted back and forth, but ultimately it would be our group to take the first bite.
