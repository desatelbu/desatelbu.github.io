Forced wipe day raiding is the fastest path to riches in Rust - and on this forced wipe, we did exactly that. It didn’t take long for us to establish a feud with another competitive group with a similar gameplan.


Provocation is batted back and forth, but ultimately it would be our group to take the first bite.
