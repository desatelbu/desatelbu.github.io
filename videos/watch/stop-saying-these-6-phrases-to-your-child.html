Be careful of what you say to your kids

Kids are affected by what you say – more than they care to show

That is why it is often said that one should be mindful and cautious before speaking anything to your children or even infront of them

Your words shape their personality development; the wrong ones can damage their self esteem as well as set the wrong example!
