Thailand is known for its best street food. Street food in Thailand can be found all over the country, in both cities and in rural areas. It brings people together and is also an important part of Thai culture. The Thai food sold at this street food night market today ranges from soups, curries, noodles like khanom chin, to the fish dishes like the ubiquitous som tam for example.
