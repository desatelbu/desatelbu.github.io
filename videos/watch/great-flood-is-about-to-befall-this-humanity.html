My daughter, thank you for listening to my call in your heart. Cry out and say with determination that the great flood is about to befall this humanity,
during which a great Cross will be seen by the whole world. Convert urgently.

everyone will understand, at least those who had a firm faith based on the Word of God.
The Devil wants to succeed in implanting his mark in you. Pay attention!!!

but whoever loses his life for My sake will find it.

Our Lord Jesus to Gisella Cardia on November 21st, 2021:

My brothers and sisters, thank you for having consoled Me again today with your presence. Brothers and sisters,
My children, you who ask Me every day in your hearts if you are doing enough for Me,
I tell you in truth: every time I hear your voices singing praises and prayers,
My angels join with you so that Heaven and Earth become one.
My brothers and sisters, to do My Will is to love one another in truth; be brothers and sisters, united as My people should be:
walking and following in My footsteps on the way of mutual love and truth.
I will be with you until the end of Holy Mass to bless you one by one. Peace be with you. Your Jesus…
