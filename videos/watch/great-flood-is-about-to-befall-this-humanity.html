My brothers and sisters, to do My Will is to love one another in truth; be brothers and sisters, united as My people should be:
walking and following in My footsteps on the way of mutual love and truth.
I will be with you until the end of Holy Mass to bless you one by one. Peace be with you. Your Jesus…
