A image is being shared on social media that claims to show Brian Laundrie’s mom Roberta dressed in a black dress and hat the day before his remains were identified by investigators. The clip, first shared to TikTok on October 20, purports to have been captured earlier the same day by a neighbor living across from the Laundrie family in North Port, Florida. On the morning of October 20, Roberta Laundrie and her husband Chris joined investigators on a search of Myakkahatchee Creek Park where they discovered a bag belonging to their son just 90 minutes after arriving. The miraculous discovery, which came after a five-week search by local police and the FBI, was made shortly before investigators located skeletal remains next to Brian’s backpack and notebook. The Laundries were captured on video by a Fox News reporter being told by a police officer "I think we found something.
