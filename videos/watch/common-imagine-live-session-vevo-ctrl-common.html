Common’s ‘A Beautiful Revolution, Part 2’ album teems with big ideas, great grooves, and fierce flows. Cultural pride and wishes for a better life for any of us who may be feeling downpressed in these chaotic times. “Imagine” is response to such pressure, a wish or a prayer that better days come soon. Imagine layers in the game where we all players/No more stargazing or police car chasing/Imagine life that bring us Lauryn Hill type of singers…” The Chicago hip-hop icon, who’s been a longtime Vevo bud, is always speaks from a balance of heart and mind, and “Imagine” has the power to whisk you away for few minutes – a valuable gift for anyone in 2021. It was big treat to have the maestro hit our Brooklyn studios and spit this one. Heads up, greatness is in the house.
