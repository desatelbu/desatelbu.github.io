Join us LIVE for an inspiring event benefiting EB Research Partnership’s mission to cure Epidermolysis Bullosa (EB). Donations can be made at give.ebresearch.org/ventureintocures or by texting CURE EB to 24365. Crypto donations can be made at ebresearch.org/cryptocurrency.
Host Tom Holland and The Brothers Trust have joined EBRP Co-Founders Jill and Eddie Vedder to help #healEB. Venture Into Cures will feature uplifting stories of individuals with EB and their families along with appearances and performances by celebrity friends Fred Armisen, James Arthur, Mayim Bialik, Jack Black, Carrie Brownstein, Brandi Carlile, Sofia Carson, Choir!Choir!Choir!, Pete Davidson, Robert Downey Jr., Lily Hevesh, Tom Holland, The Finns, Selena Gomez, Gonzo from The Muppets, Jim Jefferies, Jack Johnson, The Jonas Brothers, Charlotte Lawrence, Bill Maher, Gaten Matarazzo, Lamorne Morris, Catherine O’Hara, Ed O’Neill, Pearl Jam, Ed Sheeran, Martin Short, Scott Van Pelt, Harper, Jill and Eddie Vedder, Zendaya.
