This is a video of Parrot Wizard webinar students of the German Flight Club demonstrating how eagerly their parrots put on an Aviator Harness! After completing the "Flight Attendant" Aviator Harness training course, they were able to teach their parrots to skillfully and willingly put on the harness to go outside. Watch how well a Blue and Gold Macaw, Green Winged Macaw, African Grey, Amazon, and Senegal Parrot put on their Aviator Harnesses. This video was recorded at the 2018 annual convention of the German Aviator Club near Frankfurt.
