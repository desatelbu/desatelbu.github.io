This video has been created to promote and disseminate MEDINELINGUA which is an excellent website created by Charité Universitätsmedizin Berlin (http://medine2.com/Public/medinelingua.html).

The MEDINELINGUA website concentrates a set of resources for learning medical language in many different European laguages. It becomes an excellent tool to prepare a succesful Erasmus on any health sciences field but also to facilitate better communication between patients, travellers and health professionals across Europe.

This video has been produced by medical students of the Rovira i Virgili University in the framework of MEDINE2, the thematic network of medical education in Europe (medine2.com).
