Today we’re exploring NCT WayV. We’re doinga WayV Marathon for - Regular, Take Off, Back To You, See The V. We’ve reacted to NCT Dream, NCT U, WayV and NCT 127. WayV is a Chinese boy band which serves as the fourth sub-unit and China-based unit of the South Korean boy band NCT managed by SM Entertainment’s Chinese sub-label, Label V. The group debuted on January 17, 2019, with their digital extended play The Vision, and is composed of seven members: Kun, Ten, Winwin, Lucas, Xiaojun, Hendery and Yangyang.
