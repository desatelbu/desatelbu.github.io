Where Success Stories Begin is a series of short videos that teach children how to get on a path to success at school, at home and in life. This segment discusses the importance of making a good first impression. Making a poor first impression can make you and outcast or a target for bullies, but making a good first impression helps you make friends and earn respect. This video explains exactly how to make a good first impression. It also includes examples that show how your actions determine whether you make a good first impression or a bad one.
