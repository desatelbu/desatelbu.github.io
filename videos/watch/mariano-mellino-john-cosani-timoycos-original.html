Release Info:

Argentinian rising stars Mariano Mellino and John Cosani debut in Timeless Moment with Timoycos.

Mariano Mellino e John Cosani have been storming the progressive house scene in the last few years with releases in legendary labels such as Sudbeat , Balance and Selador, so when you join both together you can only expect pure fire. And this is exactly what Timoycos is. A very dark prog single, with lots of ambience and a unique melodic glide pitched synth that is sure to put the dance floor in a hypnotic state.

On the remix duties you have label boss Morttagua with his interpretation, bringing a tech prog vibe to Timoycos. Morttagua brings a solid groove with very elaborate percussion work, a strong bass line and distorted metallic synths to make Timoycos a pure dance floor secret weapon.

To finish this EP , Mariano Mellino brings a solo atmospheric B side single entitled Yougen. An introspective journey with lots of atmospheres and lush pads embedded in pure melodic harmony! Simply timeless!
