The apartment, at the request of the owners, was designed by RiFRALAB, RiFRAâ€™s in-house design studio. The tones of the house are developed in three colors: black, white and dove grey.

The kitchen chosen is the kitchen model ONE of RiFRA, in white matte lacquer finish.
