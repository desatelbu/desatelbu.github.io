A project characterized by a contemporary dynamism, whose owners, a young couple, have contacted RiFRA with a precise idea: a design and timeless house.

It is a two-level house, the first level where the residential part is developed, while the second level is on the terrace of the building that hosts the home and is dedicated to the wellness area and includes the solarium, an outdoor swimming pool and a mini-pool with Jacuzzi.

The apartment, at the request of the owners, was designed by RiFRALAB, RiFRA‚Äôs in-house design studio. The tones of the house are developed in three colors: black, white and dove grey.

The floor was made of black resin, with strips of LED lights inserted to delimit the area of the iron staircase leading to the terrace, while the tones of dove grey and white are developed on the vertical walls and ceiling.

The kitchen chosen is the kitchen model ONE of RiFRA, in white matte lacquer finish.
