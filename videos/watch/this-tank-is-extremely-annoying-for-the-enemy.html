The Wiesel was introduced with Ground Breaking and this version might not be as potent as the Wiesel 1A2 TOW but it’s sure as heck fun to play!!!

In this video I take the Wiesel to higher tiers so I can maximise the amount of fun I had!

I can highly recommend this little thing to be a complete troll on the battlefield! :)
