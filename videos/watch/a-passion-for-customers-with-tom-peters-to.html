Tom Peters gets to the heart of the "excellence message" by taking you on location to five leading organizations that provide superior customer service. In A Passion for Customers, Tom Peters takes you on a guided tour of five organizations:

Louisville Redbirds
Federal Express
Worthington Industries
The Limited
