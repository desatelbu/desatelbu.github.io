This video is a collaboration with Teresa of Our Greene Acres and Julie of Julie’s Designs and Signs. We each create 10 beautiful Christmas decor items using thrift store finds that cost less than $5 each. My projects include a faux birch table, Putz house bookends, a Christmas silhouette, botanical wall art, snowmen from wood salt and pepper shakers, a Christmas book diorama, a mantel clock diorama, a wooden horse on a wagon, a centerpiece from glass light shades,and a refreshed ceramic snowman. I hope you are inspired to create some budget friendly holiday decor from your thrift store finds.
