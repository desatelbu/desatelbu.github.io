Selling your home for cash has long been an option—if you find the right buyer. But these days, pretty much anyone can get a cash offer on their home with the click of a button.

In this video, we answer the question, "What is an, iBuyer?" (sometimes called an "ebuyer"), plus talk about the companies that buy houses for cash, and the benefits (and drawbacks) to selling to one of these iBuyers.

Plus, we take a quick detour to discuss the big development with iBuyers: Zillow stops buying houses.
