Hedging with futures may help reduce risk in a portfolio by potentially offsetting losses during market downswings. The leverage that futures offer makes them an excellent vehicle for hedging because a small investment allows you to control a large asset. In this video, you’ll learn how to hedge your portfolio using futures.
