On this day the swell was relatively large. Searched for a location that was barely fishable and it payed off. Had to keep a very close eye on the swell and fish correctly. it only takes one good fish on the outing and this was it!
Sydney rocks can be challenging as they are heavily fished in most locations. Hope you enjoy this video.

The outfit used on the outing:

• Rod- Daiwa Over There 109MH 328cm 10-20lb.
• Reel- Daiwa 5000D H BG MQ.
• Line- 20 lb J Braid Grand.
• Leader- 20lb J Thread FC
• Hobie Life jacket 50+
