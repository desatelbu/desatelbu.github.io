Actor Sienna Miller has settled a claim for ‘substantial damages’ against publishers of The Sun.

Ms Miller claimed her voicemails were hacked and private information misused. News Group Newspapers denied the charges and settled the case without any admission of liability.

But speaking outside court Ms Miller said she had wanted to pursue the case to a full trial but "legal recourse is not available" to people without "countless millions".
