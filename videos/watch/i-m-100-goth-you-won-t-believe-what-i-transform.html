19-YEAR-OLD Bug from Massachusetts started dressing alternatively at the age of 15 - by 18, they had turned into a full-fledged goth. Bug mostly dresses in a trad goth style but is also inspired by mall goth and punk. Bug is a natural blonde and only wears black, styling their backcombed and undercut hair into a ‘death hawk’. How will Bug’s mum react when they take on a preppier look for the day?
