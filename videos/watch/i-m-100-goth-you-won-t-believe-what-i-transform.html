19-YEAR-OLD Bug from Massachusetts started dressing alternatively at the age of 15 - by 18, they had turned into a full-fledged goth. Bug mostly dresses in a trad goth style but is also inspired by mall goth and punk. The only one in their suburban town that dresses this way, Bug lives with their mum who is pretty supportive of their style as she used to wear grunge clothes herself; Bug even inherited her leather grunge jacket. Bug is a natural blonde and only wears black, styling their backcombed and undercut hair into a ‘death hawk’. How will Bug’s mum react when they take on a preppier look for the day?
