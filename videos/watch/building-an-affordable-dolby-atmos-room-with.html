What is Dolby Atmos?

Dolby Atmos goes beyond the ordinary listening experience and puts you inside the song in a new spatial way, revealing every detail of the music with unparalleled clarity and depth.

How does Dolby Atmos work?

It starts with the artist. Dolby Atmos technology lets them place each voice, instrument, or sound in its own space. Wherever you hear it, you’re in the center.

Dolby Atmos is amazing new technology that really allows listeners, producers, mixers, and more experience music like never before. The only problem? It can definitely get very expensive to set up.

Today, along with our friends from Kali Audio, we are taking you through how you can create your own Dolby Atmos room on a budget!
