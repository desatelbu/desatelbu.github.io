I’m the first to admit that I’m a little bit OCD when it comes to maintenance - like knowing the Hilux has fresh oil, clean filters and is generally up to scratch. As I’ve mentioned before, we don’t really plan our trips - knowing the truck is ready to go at a moment’s notice helps make our spontaneous trips so much easier!

In this video I run through my quick check after each day on a trip and then jump into freshening everything up!
