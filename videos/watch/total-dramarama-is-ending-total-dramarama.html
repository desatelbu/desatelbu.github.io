Total DramaRama, the baby reboot of Total Drama Island, is ending in 2022. Episodes began airing on Cartoon Network a few years ago, but a Total DramaRama character designer has revealed that we’re about to be in the final batch of stories. What comes next for Total Drama?

As it turns out, Total Drama Island is returning in a revival for Cartoon Network and HBO Max. With classic contestants like Owen, Beth, and Harold making a comeback, along with Chris McLean as a host, this is for the original fans of Total Drama Island!
