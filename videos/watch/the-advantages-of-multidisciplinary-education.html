Successful people often have a story of difficulties and number of failures behind them. But is there really no success without struggle? In this motivating and informative speech Ammar Younas will share tips based on his personal experience that led him to triumph. He will also shed a light on his journey to pursue a multi-disciplinary educational background and to his formula to become a champion student. Ammar Younas is a Visiting Professor at Tashkent State University of Law. At the early age of 21 years, he earned his Doctor of Medicine Degree (MD), making him one of the youngest medical doctors in the world. He holds 6 other degrees in Finance, Political Marketing, International Relations, Jurisprudence, Human Rights and Chinese Law from Kyrgyzstan, Italy, Lebanon and China. He completed both of his law degrees at Bachelor and Master levels with scholarship and as a top student of his class. Currently, he is affiliated with School of Humanities, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences Beijing as ANSO Scholar and as a Researcher at Institute of Automation at Chinese Academy of Sciences China. Besides his 5 books, 2 patents and numerous articles in academic and journalistic medias, he speaks 6 languages, holds memberships of leading professional organizations, listed on the panel of various arbitration institutes and is regularly invited to speak at International Conferences, Seminars, and Webinars worldwide. Ammar Younas is an authority on Central Asian Technology Law and a pioneer of Technology Law as an academic discipline in Central Asia.
