The Reef Cup is the most important submarine race in the world. Colorín and his friends Burbujas are at the start line next to Calambres, the champion from the last few years. They will start the race together with the hopes of winning, but during the course they will have some difficulties that separate Burbujas from his dream and will put their friendship and loyalty to test. A nice story to learn that winning isn’t everything.
