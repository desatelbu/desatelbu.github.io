Replica material is great stuff, giving you access to spots that your gauge can’t reach. But 4D InSpec’s handheld gauges reach more spots. And they produce more reliable numbers, because you can see all the high and low points in a single shot.
Jared Wheeler compares the measurement results and the measurement process between a snapshot device and a replica cast from a scratch on an aviation turbine blade. You can see a difference in time, convenience and certainty.
