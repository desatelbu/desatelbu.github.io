Zayn Malik is speaking out after being accused of striking Yolanda Hadid, the mother of his girlfriend, Gigi. A source tells ET, ‘Yolanda and Zayn had a disagreement that turned hostile. He wrote in part, ‘As you all know I am a private person and I very much want to create a safe and private space for my daughter to grow up in. A place where private family matters aren’t thrown on the world stage for all to poke and pick apart. In an effort to protect that space for her I agreed to not contest claims arising from an argument I had with a family member of my partner’s who entered our home while my partner was away several weeks ago’
