Today we have a special episode of Experts Try with Travis Haley, Firearms Expert, and Cameron Fath, Spec Ops testing out Call Of Duty guns in real life. Initially, the guys will examine different weapons and their configurations to get a feel and understand the practicality of each. We then see them take those certain weapons out into a secluded area and test them out. We’ve tried to get a wide array of weapons to try out, from regular firearms to the not-so-regular-like grenade launchers. We hope you enjoy today’s episode and remember this video has been performed by trained professionals do not try this at home!

Developed and published by Activision, Call of Duty is a first-person shooter video game franchise. With its beginnings in 2003, the series focused initially on World War II-themed games. Over the years, the series has seen games set in the midst of the Cold War, futuristic worlds, and outer space.﻿
