Hello! Thank you so much to everyone for watching my Cape Wrath Series so far, here is EP4 where I hike from Kinloch Hourne after wild camping nearby and heading onto Shiel Bridge to stay the night and finally have a wash haha! After, I set off from Morvich to take on the Falls Of Glomach ( one of Britain’s tallest waterfalls) I take a long route onto the lovely, remote Bothy which is Maol Bhuidhe, enjoying a cozy evening with others who are hiking the CWT also. The following day brings me to Craig, after savage conditions with a storm rolling in - I was lucky enough to get permission to camp in the Youth Hostel haha! Some great hiking for these past days, through remote sections of the Scottish Highlands - I hope you enjoy :)

Thank you for watching! x
