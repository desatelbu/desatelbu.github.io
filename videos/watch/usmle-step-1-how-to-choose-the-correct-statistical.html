A number of participants who were administered the medication reported that they had experienced significant nausea from the medication, and rated it as mild, moderate, or severe. Moderate Nausea – 30% in treatment group, 40% in control group
Severe Nausea – 50% in treatment group, 20% in control group

- ANOVA (analysis of variance)
- Paired t-test
- Unpaired t-test
- Multiple linear regression
- Multiple logical regression
- Chi-Square test
- Pearson Correlation Coefficient
