This is probably the easiest extreme demon in GD. This took me from start to finish, around an hour and 20 minutes, and about 1100 attempts. There is plenty of room for mistakes, and there is a lot of leeway in general. Some parts are a bit tricky, such as the ball part at 50%, and the first cube, but other than that, this level is very easy. Perfect first extreme demon material in my opinion!!
