November 2021 Times

The sunrise safari starts at 05:30 local time, which means start times of 22:30 EST, 19:30 PST, 03:30 in the UK [GMT], 04:30 in Central Europe, and 14:30 Sydney time.

The sunset safari starts at 15:30 CAT local time, 08:30 EST, 05:30 PST, 13:30 in the UK [GMT], 14:30 in Central Europe, and 00:30 Sydney time.

When our safari isn’t live, you can view the feed from the Djuma Waterhole camera, from the heart of Djuma Private Game Reserve in the Sabi Sand/greater Kruger National Park area.
