Who is wise? The one who sees the consequences of his actions. Rabbi Berel Lazar.
At first glance, sin can seem like something sweet and alluring. But, no matter how it may seem at first, the consequences are always grave.

Pleasure comes in two types. The first type is the kind that leaves behind regret for days and weeks. The second type is the kind that comes as a consequence of proper prioritization and lasts much longer. We reflect on this in today’s video with Rabbi Berl Lazar.
