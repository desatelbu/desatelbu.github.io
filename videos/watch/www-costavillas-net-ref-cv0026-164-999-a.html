A beautifully presented south west facing detached villa which in recent years has undergone some refurbishment.
This spacious property boasts a sunny south facing terrace into the lounge/diner with patio doors onto a “Juliet” balcony. The lounge leads into the fabulous kitchen with integrated appliances, lots of storage, and a breakfast bar. Also on this ground floor is a W.C. On the first floor there is a bathroom and two double bedrooms with fitted wardrobes and patio doors which access a balcony overlooking landscaped parkland. There is also a utility room / hobby room which could be converted to another bedroom if required. A door accesses the rear terrace and storage. On the top floor there is a modern shower room, a walk in wardrobe and the master bedroom enjoying distant sea views. Outside, and to the front there is a sunny patio garden with established plants. However, there is sufficient room for a splash pool if required. The villa also boasts fly screens, is part air conditioned, and is to be sold part furnished. The villa is walking distance to some amenities and is only 7 minutes drive to the nearest beaches. Community fees: €149, SUMA: €285
