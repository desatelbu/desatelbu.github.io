- 2 tablespoons oyster sauce - 28g
- 1 tablespoon mirin - 14g
- 2 tablespoons shirodashi- -24g
- 1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar -12g
- 2 tablesopons - 35g (we used palm sugar)
