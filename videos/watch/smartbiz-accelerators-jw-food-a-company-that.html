JW FOOD (제이더블유푸드)

JW FOOD is a company that specializes in processing high-quality marine products under the hygienic environment. The firm has produced processed marine products with an aim of providing healthy food. The company has produced processed marine products including mackerel, sole and frozen pollack which can be enjoyed conveniently. “Norway mackerel fillet” which is aged with herbs is the main product of the company. Different types of fishes are caught in season and they are processed into convenient products. Therefore, they can be enjoyed anytime, at any place. We introduce JW FOOD, which has enriched the tables of consumers by providing fresh marine products.

For more information on the company: jinwoo3484@naver.com
