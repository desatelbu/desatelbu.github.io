How to build a small house cheap?
Hi. In this video series, we’ll show you the detailed construction of a small home. All construction will be carried out as simply and economically as possible in order to keep within the minimum budget.
We are building this house for our elderly parents. The total area of the house is 640 square feet and the exterior dimensions are 18 by 22 feet.
In this first part, we will show you how to make a simple yet reliable foundation.
The foundation materials cost us less than $ 1,000.
We bought a plot of land for $ 2,500.
The associated costs were $ 500.
At this stage, the total construction costs are $ 4,000.
