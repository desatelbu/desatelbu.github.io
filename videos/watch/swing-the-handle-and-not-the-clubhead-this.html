When you think about the golf swing like this, you are going to instantly improve your ball striking. This golf tip is going to show you how to swing the golf club correctly by changing your focus from "swinging the clubhead" to "swinging the handle". All pro golfers "swing the handle" of the golf club - so it’s no wonder that most amateur golfers struggle because they "swing the clubhead".

Change how you think about the golf swing in this golf tip and watch your ball striking improve fast.
