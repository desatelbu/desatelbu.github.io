I tried to make my own #diy saw blades in the shop with what I have available. It was quite a task to manage to make a cut with a 3D printed blade and I had to even TIG weld my own miter saw to hold a large 3D printed blade. Machining an aluminium blade was a way better choice and I manage to do a cut quite easily, I truly think that in case of need I could quickly cut a blade out of aluminium as I did in this video.
