YouTuber and commentator Daisy Cousens says there are "three reasons" why race was not mentioned in the media about the Waukesha Christmas parade tragedy.

“The first is that if the mainstream media were to mention race in this, then they would be called racist,” Ms Cousens told Sky News host Andrew Bolt.

“The second reason is if they were to mention race, they would inevitably have to mention Black Lives Matter and that ideology.

“The third reason and it is the inescapable coincidence that the Kyle Rittenhouse verdict came out just a few days before."
