The Cathedral of St. Vincent de Paul - is a Roman Catholic church located in Tunis, Tunisia. The cathedral is dedicated to Saint Vincent de Paul, patron saint of charity. Construction began in 1893 and the church was opened at Christmas 1897, albeit without its belltowers owing to a shortage of funds.
