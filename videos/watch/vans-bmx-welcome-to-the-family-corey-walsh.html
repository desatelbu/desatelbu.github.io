Vans is proud to welcome Corey Walsh to the Global BMX Family. Corey’s think-outside-the-box approach has captured the attention of the BMX community the world over. A part of the Vans Canada team since 2015, he’s made a name for himself stacking Fast & Loose and Cult clips, and recently speaking out on behalf of the LGBTQ+ community.
