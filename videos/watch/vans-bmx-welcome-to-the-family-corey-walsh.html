Vans is proud to welcome Corey Walsh to the Global BMX Family. Corey’s think-outside-the-box approach has captured the attention of the BMX community the world over. His riding is unmatched in the pool and bowl game, earning him two Transition of the Year awards, voted on by his peers. A part of the Vans Canada team since 2015, he’s made a name for himself stacking Fast & Loose and Cult clips, and recently speaking out on behalf of the LGBTQ+ community.
