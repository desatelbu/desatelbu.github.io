THE TRAVEL VLOGS ARE BACK!

This is EPISODE 1 of my solo travel vlog series to Germany via the Vaccinated Travel Lane (VTL), from Singapore. Just being in Changi Airport to board a flight for the first time in 2 years, felt extremely surreal and it took awhile for me to digest the reality of flying again.
