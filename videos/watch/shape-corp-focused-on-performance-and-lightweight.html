Shape Corp. is a full service supplier of custom metal and plastic solutions. Process and material advancements position the company as a pioneer in roll forming and injection molding. Shape serves an array of industries including automotive, office furniture, medical, agriculture and more. Founded in 1974, the company remains a privately owned, family company. Shape employs more than 3,000 associates in North America, Europe and Asia.
