Warren Buffett gives his greatest advice to students. The legendary investor shares the experience he gained over an incredible 70-year period in the stock market the hard way so that the audience can get ahead by making fewer mistakes. Mr. Buffett explains how to invest to outperform the stock market, gives some investment tips, elaborates on the American economy, answers good questions, shares his thoughts on philanthropy and many more exciting topics. The speech took place in Georgetown University (2013). This is life-changing wisdom and one of his greatest interviews ever. Hope you find a lot of value for yourself in these 50 minutes spent with this wonderful human being!

Warren E. Buffett is an American long-term investor, philanthropist, business tycoon, and the chairman & CEO of Berkshire Hathaway. He is considered one of the most successful investors in the world and has a net worth over 100 billion dollars. Buffett was born in Omaha, Nebraska. He developed an interest in business and investing in his youth and made truly incredible stock market returns over his career.
