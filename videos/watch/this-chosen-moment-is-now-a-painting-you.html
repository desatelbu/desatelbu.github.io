“You are mine.” When Jesus spoke those words to Mary Magdalene at the end of Episode One, millions around the world like you felt He was speaking to them. And the image of Mary surrendering into Jesus’ arms remains the single most impactful moment in the show’s history.

Renowned painter Liz Lemon Swindle says, “I was her…that could be me at so many different stages in my life.” Which is why this moment is now an extraordinary painting.

Hear the story of how this painting came to life and why we believe it can be healing and inspiring for people all over the world.
