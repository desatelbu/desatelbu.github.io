DRIVING HOME TO DAVAO…

It was a #BecomingFilipino car and scooter road trip with Madman and Jandee! We started in Cagayan de Oro, stopped at a old Philippines jail that is now a restaurant in Malaybalay City, carried on through Bukidnon, and ended up at Kojo Hotel in Tagum. Always a wonderful feeling to be heading back to Davao. Mindanao truly is my home here in the Philippines, and every time I road trip across it…

Positive and beautiful vibes!
