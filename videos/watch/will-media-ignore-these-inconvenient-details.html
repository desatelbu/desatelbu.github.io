First, Dave goes into the details about the suspect in the Waukesha Christmas parade incident that you won’t be hearing on CNN or the mainstream media. Dave also shares a clip of Winsome Sears’ fiery response to being asked if she was vaccinated by a CNN host. How many COVID booster shots will Americans need to get? Dr. Fauci is unsure, but he has now admitted that it could be possible that a COVID 19 booster shot would be required every six months. Finally, Dave shares a clip from the Ben Shapiro Sunday Special of Florida Governor Ron DeSantis giving the blueprint for how Republicans need to deal with the media bias of the liberal media.
