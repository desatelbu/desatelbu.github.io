First, Dave goes into the details about the suspect in the Waukesha Christmas parade incident that you won’t be hearing on CNN or the mainstream media. Dr. Fauci is unsure, but he has now admitted that it could be possible that a COVID 19 booster shot would be required every six months. Finally, Dave shares a clip from the Ben Shapiro Sunday Special of Florida Governor Ron DeSantis giving the blueprint for how Republicans need to deal with the media bias of the liberal media.
