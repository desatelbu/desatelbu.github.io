Dave also shares a clip of Winsome Sears’ fiery response to being asked if she was vaccinated by a CNN host. How many COVID booster shots will Americans need to get? Finally, Dave shares a clip from the Ben Shapiro Sunday Special of Florida Governor Ron DeSantis giving the blueprint for how Republicans need to deal with the media bias of the liberal media.
