Recently, the Chinese government is taking even tighter control over the media and banned private capitals from investing in news related business. So basically, only the Chinese government’s mouthpieces are allowed to report news now. It is well known that the Chinese media never had much “independence” or “freedom” so to speak. So why is the Chinese government now making it clear and taking out the last shred of “independence” in the media?
