Today’s Episode 240 – Engaging Your Body In Worship with hosts Jason Wieland and Linda Tokar talks about how we are called to surrender ourselves wholly (including our bodies) to God in worship. This episode looks at ways we can engage our bodies in acts of worship and how we can practice certain physical disciplines as worship.
