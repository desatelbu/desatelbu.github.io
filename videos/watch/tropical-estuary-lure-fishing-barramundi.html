Neap tides a week out from the barra closure, Dave and I decided to go looking for more current and hit up a smaller local estuary in search for some Barramundi and Mangrove Jack.

Fishing with hardbody lures we snaked our way through multiple small creeks casting into snags and shade pockets looking for our targets.

All in all an epic day on the water with plenty of fish and even more laughs.

Tackle used.
Rod - Dobyns Champion Extreme 702c
Reel - Conquest 200HG
Braid - 20lb Sunline Castaway
Leader - 40lb Sunline FC Rock
Lure - Lucky Craft Pointer 78XD
Hooks - BKK Viper #6
