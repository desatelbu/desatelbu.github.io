Vehicles in the game consist of a soft-body node-beam structure similar to those in Rigs of Rods. In terms of soft-body physics, vehicles realistically flex and deform as stresses to the skeleton, such as impacts from collisions, are applied. Aside from body deformation, various other types of damage are simulated such as degraded engine, detached doors and shattered windows.
