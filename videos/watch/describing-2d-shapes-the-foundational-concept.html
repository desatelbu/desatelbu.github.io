The foundational concept of two dimensional shapes is introduced in an easy to understand manner using familiar everyday objects to illustrate the most common 2D shapes. Squares, rhombuses, triangles, kites, circles, rectangles… they’re all there! Also included is a short introduction to quadrilaterals, and the definitions of sides and corners.
