The SB02 from the French company Sartory Billard is unique in that the watch is customizable by changing out the bezel on the top of the watch. 

Sartory Billard offers bezels for the SB02 in a variety of colors and finishes and even some that are hand-engraved. In this in-depth video review, I’ll show a couple of different bezel and strap options and discus the unique case design of the Sartory Billard SB02.

Prices for the SB02 start at 542 Euros including VAT

For more information, visit www.sartory-billard.com
