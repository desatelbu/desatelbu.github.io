From sports and sneakers, with Nike, Adidas, Puma, Converse to designer drip from Versace and Balenciaga. Every fashion brand comes with an iconic logo. However many other icons, such as the Nike Swoosh, the Le Coq Sportif rooster and the Puma have changed. Many have evolved a lot over the years.
