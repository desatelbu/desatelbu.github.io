You can now be able to get your cargo imported from Dubai to Nairobi within 3 days.africa freight systems, a subsidiary of Dubai based freight and logistics company freight systems will also offer traders with credit of advance cash to top up purchase of goods and also pay for importation fee.  Speaking during a conference aimed at promoting and accelerating the cargo shipping business into Kenya and across borders by air and sea, Africa freight systems CEO UAE and Africa, Vincent Brank assured importers that the shipping firm will going forward deliver goods from Dubai to Kenya in 3 days by air and 12-14 days by sea.
