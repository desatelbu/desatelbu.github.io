Professional Voice Society Thursday Webinar 08 - A Guideline on Stuttering and Cluttering - Prof.Dr. Haldun Oguz - Prof.Dr. Katrin Neumann

Professional Voice Society is celebrating its 20th anniversary. Each Thursday a webinar is performed by well-known speakers from the field. The topic of this week’s webinar was "A Guideline on Stuttering and Cluttering”. Prof.Dr. Katrin Neumann (Director of the Department of Phoniatrics and Pedaudiology at the University Clinic of Münster, Germany) was the guest of the president of our society, Prof.Dr. Haldun Oguz.
