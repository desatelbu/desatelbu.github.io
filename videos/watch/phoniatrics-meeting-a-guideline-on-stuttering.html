The topic of this week’s webinar was "A Guideline on Stuttering and Cluttering”. Prof.Dr. Katrin Neumann (Director of the Department of Phoniatrics and Pedaudiology at the University Clinic of Münster, Germany) was the guest of the president of our society, Prof.Dr. Haldun Oguz.
