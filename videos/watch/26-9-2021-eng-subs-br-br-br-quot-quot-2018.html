BREAKING NEWS.
SYRIA. TURKEY. RUSSIA.
Heavy and continuous air strikes have been launched by the Russian air force in the last twenty-four hours in many areas of northwestern Syria, where Turkish and Turkish-backed troops are based.
According to the media and mainly the website of the Syrian Observatory for Human Rights, the Russian planes also carried out attacks in the province of Afrin, northwest of Aleppo, in the area that is under the control of Turkey, after its invasion, with the "Olive Branch" operation, which was conducted in 2018 and they write that the target was a military headquarters and that so far there are 4 victims, who were members of the al-Hamzah division controlled by Turkey and 15 wounded who are members of the same group and have been transported to hospitals.
In addition, the Russian forces carried out airstrikes in the areas of Baslayha and Jabal al-Ahlam, which are also located in Afrin, but also in al-Barah and Jabal al-Zawiyah in southern Idlib, where there is a Turkish observatory post, while reinforcements sent by Turkey have been deployed on the M4 highway, which connects Aleppo with Latakia.
If Mr. Erdogan wanted an answer from Russia about what is to come in Syria, he is probably taking it in the last few hours.
