About Siwas Dance Pop Revolution:
“Siwas Dance Pop Revolution” follows momager Jessalynn Siwa and her pop superstar daughter JoJo Siwa as they create and launch a brand new group called XOMG Pop, music’s next big sensation! Jess has searched the country for the most talented kid triple threats and hand selected 11 of the most talented girls to compete for a spot in this new group. These kids are vying for a shot at stardom and getting a once-in-a-lifetime chance to open a concert for none other than JoJo Siwa herself.
