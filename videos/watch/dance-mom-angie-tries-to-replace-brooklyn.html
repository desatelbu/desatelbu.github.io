About Siwas Dance Pop Revolution:
“Siwas Dance Pop Revolution” follows momager Jessalynn Siwa and her pop superstar daughter JoJo Siwa as they create and launch a brand new group called XOMG Pop, music’s next big sensation! Jess has searched the country for the most talented kid triple threats and hand selected 11 of the most talented girls to compete for a spot in this new group. With Jess as the manager, and JoJo as the choreographer and mentor, Team Siwa will put to use all of their expertise in converting raw talent and skill into a worldwide phenomenon. Over the course of 8 episodes, we will see these girls (and moms) navigate the intensity of competitions to whittle down from 11 hopefuls into the perfect girl group. These kids are vying for a shot at stardom and getting a once-in-a-lifetime chance to open a concert for none other than JoJo Siwa herself.
