Carole Robin discusses her book "Connect: Building Exceptional Relationships with Family, Friends, and Colleagues." The ability to create strong relationships with others is crucial to living a full life and becoming more effective at work. Yet many of us find ourselves struggling to build solid personal and professional connections or unable to handle challenges that inevitably arise when we grow closer to others.
