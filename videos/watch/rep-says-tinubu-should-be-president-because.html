In the aftermath of the recent police brutality witnessed during the one-year anniversary of the #EndSARS protests, victims are towing the legal route to get justice. #AdedotunClement, an Uber driver that was assaulted by some officers of the Lagos State Neighborhood Safety Corps, and the Nigeria Police Force Are demanding the sum of Five Hundred Million Naira as compensation for the gross violation of his fundamental rights to dignity of the human person, personal liberty and property, deprivation of his source of livelihood and the destruction of his vehicle.
