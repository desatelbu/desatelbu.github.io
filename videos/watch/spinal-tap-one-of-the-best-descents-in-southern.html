Spinal Tap in Richfield, Utah is not to be missed if you are in the area. Kudos to the builders of this one, the utilization of natural features is absolutely next-level. James Weingarten and I had great time unpacking the puzzle that this one offers. If you are traveling between Moab and Saint George, this is a logical stop to spin the legs out for a couple of hours. This one can be shuttled or accessed by pedaling. #followcamfriday approved.
