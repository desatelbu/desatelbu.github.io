We are driving our bus all the way through Tennessee, and are taking our time to explore! Our goal is to drive 570 miles through Tennessee, starting in the northeast from Bristol and exiting through the southwestern city of Memphis.

In this video, we’ve made it to the Nashville area (be sure to check out part 1 to see how we got here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nCpLmYvW0UU) and are starting our journey west towards Arkansas.

We stop by a local moonshine distillery (Gutter Bound Distillery in Manchester, TN) for some a tasting of some local moonshine and a tour of the distillery and their machines.

Then, we set out to find a pumpkin patch since it’s the day before Halloween, and find an excellent place just east of Memphis, called Priddy Farms (Bartlett, TN). There we saw TONS of farm animals (pigs, goats, a llama, peacocks, rabbits, ponies, and even a donkey!!), got lost in a corn maze, and found a pumpkin to take home with us!

Finally, we drive over the Mississippi River, into Arkansas, and out of Tennessee. We made it the 570 miles, and it’s time to celebrate! We spend the evening carving a skoolie design into the pumpkin and share a pumpkin swiss roll.

We loved our trek through Tennessee, and are lucky to have been able to see so much of it!
