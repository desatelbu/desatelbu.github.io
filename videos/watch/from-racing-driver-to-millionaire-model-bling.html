RENEE Gracie, from Queensland, Australia, is living the high life after swapping a career as a professional racing driver to become a full-time glamour model. Renee began go-karting aged 13 and quickly got into competitive racing, graduating to the V8 Supercar circuit. But by the time she left racing, she was struggling to survive. Determined to make some money, Renee signed up to OnlyFans and made $24,000 in her first month - but since July 2021 she has been clearing over $100,000 a month. Earlier this year she splashed out on a million dollar mansion in her native Australia and her dream car - a Ford Mustang GT. In this episode we meet up with Renee as she gives us the house tour and goes to collect her newest purchase - a $170,000 Dodge Ram 1500 Limited SUV, freshly imported from the USA, which she’s paying to have custom-wrapped in neon yellow by the team at Gangsta Wraps.
