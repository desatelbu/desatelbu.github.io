Flight Club Fridays lands in Miami with special guest Shenseea. The Jamaican-born singer and lyricist speaks on artistic legacy, sharing “I want to make timeless music because people who I looked up to made timeless music. That’s how music lives on.” Breaking down her songwriting process, Shenseea emphasizes her commitment to quality and elaborates on her mission to empower women through her lyrics. Featuring two of her hit singles Blessed and Rebel, along with a special performance of Ok Ok pt 2 from Kanye West’s Grammy nominated project Donda.

Flight Club Fridays is a selection of performances recorded live at Flight Club NYC, LA and Miami in celebration of music and community.
