Three months after making an amazing solo journey to the Wild is Life-IFAW boma (home) at Panda Masuie, Moses is thriving with his new herd. The other rescue elephants have taken him under their wing, including Nkanyezi, who has become like Moses’s adopted mother. Moses will remain in care at Panda Masuie with the hopes of eventually being released back into the wild. #Elephants #WildlifeRescue #Conservation
