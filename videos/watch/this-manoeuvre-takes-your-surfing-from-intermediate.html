In this surfing lesson I go through some optimal techniques, both on the forehand and backhand, for how to do re-entries in the surf. Re-entries are section-dependent, and they are a hallmark of a surfer who has the potential to progress to an advanced level.

If you want to learn how to surf at an intermediate, or even at advanced level, this video is for you.
