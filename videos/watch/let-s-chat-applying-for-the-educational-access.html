The Educational Access Scheme (EAS) is for students who have experienced long-term disadvantage(s) and may help with entrance into university. We give you an overview of EAS and discuss:
– categories of disadvantage
– how to apply
– application documentation
– EAS key dates (have changed since this webinar was published please refer to UAC’s Key Dates)
– application tips.

And we give answers to the most commonly asked questions!

Find out more information on our website: https://www.uac.edu.au/future-applicants/scholarships-and-schemes/educational-access-schemes
