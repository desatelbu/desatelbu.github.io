Ship Charterer

As a child, Audrey Wu was inspired by her father who used to work at the port. Now a Ship Charterer with Pacific Carriers Limited, Audrey ensures that her ships are kept busy all year round, and that cargoes are shipped safely. Part of her role includes fixing charters for her clients and keeping abreast of geopolitical changes.
