Following their compelling debut ‘List’, which saw support from Sian Eleri at BBC Radio 1, French producer duo il:lo release their follow-up ‘Cesta’, out July 30. Featuring a soothing vocal amongst a bed of melodic plucks, ‘Cesta’ is reminiscent of the chimerical atmospheres of Hiatus, Koresma, and Tycho.

Forming in 2012, il:lo have released on Future Classic, bitbird, Future Archive Recordings and Loci Records, releasing several EPs and remixing the likes of Panama, Jerro, Emancipator and CloZee. Characterised by extensive use of intricate sampling and live instrumentation, il:lo’s whimsical productions are inspired from the duo’s travels, taking their names from valued moments in their journeys.

‘Cesta’ is the second of three singles to be released on Anjunadeep this year.
