We’ve teamed up and created a 100 Thieves x Higround keyboard, jelly bag, and hoodie. In this video, I’m going to unbox the seeding kit with Higround founders, Rustin and Kha. This has been in the works for over a year and I couldn’t be more excited for the future.

The keyboard, jelly bag, and hoodie go on sale October 15th @ 12PM PST at https://higround.co
