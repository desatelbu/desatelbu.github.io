Zander The Coach of Coaches & now Author,  stops by HQ before the release of his book to talk about his life’s work that lead him to his book “Shit You Don’t Learn In College”. Bedros and Zander give high level and micro level insight, on our internal and external conversations, which lead us to the crossroads of action, and how that action provides us data to become better. But what stops most is the inner battle between our own two ears, and through their own success, they give all the knowledge necessary on what it truly takes to achieve your goals. This one is heavy note taking, so be ready and enjoy this eye-opening conversation!
