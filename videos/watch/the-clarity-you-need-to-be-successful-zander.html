Zander The Coach of Coaches & now Author,  stops by HQ before the release of his book to talk about his life’s work that lead him to his book “Shit You Don’t Learn In College”. Bedros and Zander give high level and micro level insight, on our internal and external conversations, which lead us to the crossroads of action, and how that action provides us data to become better.
