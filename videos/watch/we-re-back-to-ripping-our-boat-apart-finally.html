Our once salvaged sailboat has been sitting on the hard through the winter. It’s summer now, and time to hit the ocean. We have a couple of big projects before it’s safe to launch again. Mainly the fact that our prop shaft was leaking and needed to be serviced.

Hard enough that the keel pivoted and pushed the aft end up into the hull. This force cracked the internal grid in several places and broke a portion of the grid free from hull.
