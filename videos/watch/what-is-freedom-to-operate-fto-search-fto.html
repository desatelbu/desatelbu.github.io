What is Freedom to operate search?

Freedom to operate (FTO) search, also known as a ‚Äúclearance search‚Äù or ‚Äúright to use‚Äù search, is a kind of search commonly performed to assess patent infringement risk upon a new product launch. Patent filing is increasing globally and before any new product is manufactured, launched, or sold in the market, it is important to thoroughly check the existing patents to be sure that none of the product components infringes upon or encroaches upon claims of any existing patents in a specific jurisdiction.
