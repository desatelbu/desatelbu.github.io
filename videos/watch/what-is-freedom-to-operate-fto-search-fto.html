What is Freedom to operate search?

Freedom to operate (FTO) search, also known as a “clearance search” or “right to use” search, is a kind of search commonly performed to assess patent infringement risk upon a new product launch. FTO analysis can save companies from many legal disputes, when it is done in advance, specifically, before the launch of the new product. Patent filing is increasing globally and before any new product is manufactured, launched, or sold in the market, it is important to thoroughly check the existing patents to be sure that none of the product components infringes upon or encroaches upon claims of any existing patents in a specific jurisdiction.

FTO search is explained step by step to provide a thorough understanding of FTO and how to do FTO search.
