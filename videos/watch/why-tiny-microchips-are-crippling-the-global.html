While the automotive industry was ravaged early on in the pandemic thanks to lockdown measures and a dramatic decrease in travel, it more recently has begun facing a new problem: a shortage of microchips. At the same time, chipmakers were experiencing supply shortages and increased demand from other sectors, such as personal electronics.
