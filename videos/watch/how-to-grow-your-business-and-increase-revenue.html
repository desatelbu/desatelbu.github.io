About Leila:

Leila Hormozi is an American entrepreneur, investor, and philanthropist. Leila started her career in the fitness industry and co-founded Gym Launch, a company valued at over $100M by the age of 27.

From there, she and her husband (Alex Hormozi) co-founded two other companies: Prestige Labs, an e-commerce supplement company, and ALAN, a software company that nurtures leads for digital marketing agencies.

After that, she ascended to a board position in each of her companies, allowing her the time to take equity positions in other businesses to help them grow. She now is partner and co-founder of Acquisition.com which acts as the holding company for all her business ventures, which at present are responsible for over $100,000,000 in yearly revenue across a variety of industries.

Leila has become known for her skills in scaling businesses through flexible infrastructures, and is frequently called upon by other business owners for her wisdom.
