Mini Apple Pie Recipe

Start with a good pie crust. This is my favorite recipe - https://www.inspiredtaste.net/22662/flaky-pie-crust-recipe/

Once it‚Äôs just starting to get a little soft turn off the heat and add a teaspoon of honey (or more depending on your variety of apples.

Fill mini pies just like you would a big pie and add the lattice top, and trim off extra.
Bake at 375 until done. I like to pull them out after about 20 minutes and add some melted butter to the top because my RV oven does not like to brown anything.
