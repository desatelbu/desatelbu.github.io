This is a video about the arrangement of the workplace for multifunctional Bench Grinders.
This time I decided to spend some time arranging my workspace in my garage. I have long planned to do something more functional with the steel cabinet on which stands my old Bench Grinder, and replace it alltogether with something more practical.
It’s been about 1.5 months since the premiere of my last video. During this time, my family and I contracted covid-19 and as a result I could not keep up my hobby during this time. However, it’s all behind now and I am excited to share with you, my newly invented and made at home product.
Stay safe and thank you all for watching!
