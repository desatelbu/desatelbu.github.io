Lateral Projection:

SID: 72 inches
X-Ray tube: No angulation
Central ray: T7 at midcoronal plane
Breathing instructions: Full inspiration
Shielding: required

Imaging details:

kVp: 100 to 110
Grid for upright bucky
Non-grid for mobile
AEC= outer chamber for PA, inner chamber for lateral

Imaging critique:

T7 at center of imaging field
Bilateral costophrenic angles and lung apexes included
Proximal clavicles equal distance to midline of thoracic spine
Scapula bodies non-superimposed lateral lung fields
10 to 11 ribs visualized superior to diaphragm
