Pierre Åström drives around in a customised Volvo FH with 540 horsepower from 2015, which is used as a tipper truck with extendable tray. Pierre’s view on customised trucks is that you have to have a lot of bling, including chromed details. It’s all about getting noticed and the job gets a lot more fun when driving around in a nice truck.
