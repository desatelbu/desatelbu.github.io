This shows that it is NOT ambient pollution that has been causing our severe air pollution problem. Rather, the source is near i.e., localized and is most probably of a single source or some sources under the command of a single man or organization. In short, we ARE BEING POISONED, locally. Neighbors with Rolls Royce don’t even realize this. This is also why people to the north don’t have the same issue, AT ALL. Their air is clean or normal. If you sit next to a bonfire of trash or ash, the PM 2.5 reading would be 40 within a meter as long as the smoke doesn’t blow into you directly. If it does, the reading goes to 600.
