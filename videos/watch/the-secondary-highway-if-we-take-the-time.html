‘If we take the time to look into our past, we make better sense of ourselves’. Robert Scott is a photographer from Alberta Canada who has been documenting endangered abandoned history on the Canadian prairies. ‘The Secondary Highway’ is a collection of vanishing stories told by a dying generation. It’s vital that we document their stories now before they disappear with the structures. ‘What started out as pictures, led to a road of self discovery down a Secondary Highway’.
