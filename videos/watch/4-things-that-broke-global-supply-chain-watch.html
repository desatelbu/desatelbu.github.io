In this video we look at four reasons for the breakdown in the global supply chain. These factors are things like The Pandemic causing a major stoppage in the flow of global shipping. Psychology is another way from the “lying flat,” or tang ping movement to van-life mentality and even people choosing family life over work in the United states. The resulting logistical bottlenecks mean that shipping containers have quadrupled in price. The final straw is the energy crisis in China caused by a shift in it’s trade with Australia and a crack down on pollution.
