All about the dragons: Red, purple, blue, green and black.

Red: Production (Resources, construction, research)

Purple: Infantry (Defense)

Blue: Cavalry (Attack)

Green: Archer (Hp)

Black: Siege

To enhance dragon skills you need dragon glasses. To evolve/unlock your dragons, you need soul stones. Both can be acquired from certain level barbarian camps.
