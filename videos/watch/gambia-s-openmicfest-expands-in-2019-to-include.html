Hip-hop is today’s soundtrack for a self-determined future, while west Africa’s traditional music reflects a universal past. AfriCourage brings both together. Starring Baaba Maal, ST, Tatadindin, Gee, Smockey, Killa Ace, Sambou Suso, the Liberation Orchestra of Inverted Traditions, the Ensemble Modern, Wimme & Rinne and more.

With the aim of introducing Gambian culture to a wider world, OpenMic introduces AfriCourage, welcoming local names, regional stars and international visitors. OpenMic and AfriCourage transform the Independence Stadium into your living room, for the biggest party in modern Gambia!
