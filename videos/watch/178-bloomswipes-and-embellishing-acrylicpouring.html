Today I’m working on a 16’’ (40cm) round canvas.
I did say I would add a photo of the pens I used for embellishing at the end, I’m sorry I forgot!
I used Krylon Rose Gold Leafing Pen, Amsterdam Acrylic Marker in Deep Gold and a Pale Lilac fine tip gel glitter pen out of a cheap set that I bought from Kmart, Australia.

My friend Jessica Winterstrom has inspired this scoop and drag bloomswipe. Jessica does some amazing work on Instagram and now has her own Youtube channel,here is the link please go and subscribe, you will love her work: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P9twcP3nYJA

Pillow/base: British Paints low sheen house paint in White, to which I added a splash of GAC800.
Colours:
#TLP (This Little Piggy) pigments in Sangria mixed with Liquitex Prizm Violet
#TLP Blue Eyes mixed with Decoart Extreme Sheen in Pearl
TLP pigments, swiping tools, silicone stir sticks and silicone pour mats can be purchased from https://fluid-art.co/
Pearlex Duotone Violet Brass
(Pearlex pigments are made by Jacquard and can be purchased worldwide).
Papermill pigments in Rose Gold (Papermill pigments can be purchased from Riot Art in Australia).
Folkart Colorshift Black Flash
Pebeo Iridescent Green Blue
All colours and pigments are mixed with untinted house paint and Jo Sonja varnish.
Please see video #92 for how I mix my colours (both pigments and tubes).
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ovlqWm_BOxw

CA/Cell Activator: Today I am using Atelier Interactive in Metallic Copper, this is only mixed with Australian Floetrol. I never measure ratios, I mix until it leaves a slight trace when drizzled from around an inch above the surface.
