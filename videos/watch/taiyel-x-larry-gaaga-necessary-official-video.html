After the release of her Ep “T617” showered with many great reviews for it’s melodic vibe and passionate singing, The fast-rising New York-Nigerian female singer and songwriter - Taiyel follows up with a brand new single “Necessary”, at a time when the voices of powerful female artist should to be heard.
The rising star Taiyel said “Necessary is a song that can take Afrobeat to the world, I wanted to also contribute to creating an Afrobeat song that any one can sing and love. I really think the universe gave me that with this beautiful song at a moment African music is speaking louder than it has ever been”
In the production of the record she is accompanied by Larry Gaaga, A Prolific producer, music creator, and multi award winning music executive. The music video is directed and shot by legendary Nigerian video director Clarence Peters of (Capital Dreams Pictures). Taiyel is no newbie to the music industry, the talent and hard work embedded in her blossomed the collaboration with Larry Gaaga. She is excited to collaborate with many new budding artists like her in 2022.
