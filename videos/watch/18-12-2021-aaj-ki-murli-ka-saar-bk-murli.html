PEACE OF MIND TV - A Brahma Kumaris TV Channel is among the most viewed devotional/spiritual TV channels in India, according to BARC data.

Through numerous social service activities and partnerships, it promotes spiritual understanding, leadership with integrity and elevated actions towards a better world.
