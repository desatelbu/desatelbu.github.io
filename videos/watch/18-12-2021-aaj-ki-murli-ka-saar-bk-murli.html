PEACE OF MIND TV - A Brahma Kumaris TV Channel is among the most viewed devotional/spiritual TV channels in India, according to BARC data.

The institution nurtures respect for all faith traditions and delineates an enlightened lifestyle. Through numerous social service activities and partnerships, it promotes spiritual understanding, leadership with integrity and elevated actions towards a better world.
