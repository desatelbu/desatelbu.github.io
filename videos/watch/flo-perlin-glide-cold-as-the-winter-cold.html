From ‘Cocooned’, Flo’s first album, ‘Glide’ make part from an incredibly personal collection of songs, many of which she wrote in her teenage years. It was then that she started to explore where her family came from and she ended up travelling around the world for two years, visiting family she had never met before, collecting pieces of her identity. From her first love to the understanding that life doesn’t always go in a straight line. Flo’s lyrics articulate something for her listeners that they’ve perhaps experienced themselves. An incredibly nostalgic album, reflecting the topic of ‘change’. With this beautiful voice and talent, Flo takes us on her journey into adulthood.
But she already has a new album call “Characters" full of new stories and her new explorations.
