WHY IS THE INTERVIEWER ASKING YOU THE QUESTION, WHY DO YOU WANT TO WORK HERE?
They want to establish whether or not you have RESEARCHED their company, and you have READ the job description because, if you haven’t done either of those things, you are less likely to stay in the job long-term.

The majority of employers want people who are going to stay working for them for a lengthy period of time, and this question assesses your MOTIVATIONS for working for their company.

Therefore, we must ensure you give a positive, and passionate answer that shows you GENUINELY want to work for their company.

THE 3 THINGS YOU MUST INCLUDE IN YOUR ANSWER TO THE INTERVIEW QUESTION, WHY DO YOU WANT TO WORK HERE?
#1. Tell the interviewer you have RESEARCHED their company and you are attracted to their HIGH STANDARDS, their company VALUES, and their eagerness to SUCCEED.

Why should we hire you for this position?

#2. Tell the interviewer you have READ THE JOB DESCRIPTION, and this job is the PERFECT FIT for your skills and qualities.

#3. Mention the company’s PRODUCTS OR SERVICES in your answer, and that you want to work for them because they take great pride in delivering outstanding customer or client service.

LET’S NOW TAKE A LOOK AT A BRILLIANT SCRIPTED ANSWER TO THE DIFFICULT INTERVIEW QUESTION: WHY DO YOU WANT TO WORK HERE? 02:12
“I want to work here because having researched your company, it is clear you take great pride in the work you do, you always strive to provide outstanding customer service, and you set high standards.

Because we spend so much time at work, I want that time to be put to good use with a positive, and proactive organization that offers great products and services, and that is constantly moving forward.

I want to work here because the job description is a match for my skills and qualities; I genuinely believe I can add value to your company, which in turn will give me great levels of job satisfaction.

Finally, working here will mean I get to work alongside other like-minded professionals who are all striving to achieve exciting things for your company.”
