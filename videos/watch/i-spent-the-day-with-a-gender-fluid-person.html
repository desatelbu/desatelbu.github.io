In this video, I spend the day with Eleven, a gender-fluid person named Eleven. Eleven is non-binary or genderqueer, which means that they do not identify as male or female, and is why they use the pronoun they/them instead of him or her.

Eleven was a beauty YouTuber named Lynette Cenée until they started going through a spiritual process that awakened this transformation. In this video, they discuss how plant medicine inspired this spiritual awakening and the journey it was to becoming Eleven.

Eleven was staying at my house for a week, so we decided to spend the day together so I can learn more about what it means to be gender fluid non-binary, and the journey it’s been to become Eleven.
