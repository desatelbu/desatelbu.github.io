► Historical Then And Now Photos, Locations and Cities
► Hello everyone and welcome back to Mystery Scoop.
In this unique video presentation, we compare historical Then and Now photos of the same locations, to observe how much the world has changed or perhaps remained the same. In some cases, the changes are enormous and almost unbelievable. The buildings, transportation, and clothing of the people appear to be vastly different. In other cases, landscapes have remained practically unchanged while people and technology have seen significant changes. Let’s have a look at a few carefully selected scenes for this video. Let us know in the comments section below which change you found most compelling.

Please check the video to see comparison photos showing how the world has changed thanks to rephotography and juxtaposed images.
