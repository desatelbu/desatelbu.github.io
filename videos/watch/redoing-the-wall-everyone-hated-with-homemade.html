In a recent kitchen reno project, I was on the hunt for a ready made can of limewash to apply to a range hood. I found it difficult to source a can in my area and took to the internet for advice. Many suggest that limewash is a fairly simple recipe that can actually be made from scratch from easy to source materials! Say no more, I had to try this out for myself. After many sample batches and different formulas tested, I’ve come up with my very own recipe that worked beautifully on a large wall in our office.
