A baby duck living with the stork family finds a magic red shoe. The peculiarity of these shoes is that they give the wearer the ability to fly. But one day the red shoes fall into the hands of the wolf and the wolf tries to steal the eggs of the stork. When the duck sees this, he is brave and starts flying for the first time and saves the eggs. The duck no longer needs magic to fly. A little courage is enough for him.
