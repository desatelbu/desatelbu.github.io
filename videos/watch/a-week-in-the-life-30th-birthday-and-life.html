Hi Everyone!

We are back after a 2 week break! Last Sunday, Megan celebrated her 30th birthday so we had a very chilled day at home with family.

So, we travelled to Norfolk to spend some time with Jamie’s parents and get additional support.

If anybody has any newborn winding techniques or tips, do let us know! We think Rupert may have colic :(

We are determined to do some singing this week or anything that isn’t baby related!
