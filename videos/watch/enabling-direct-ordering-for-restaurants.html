"Restaurants should focus on making great food, Blink can focus on building your online ordering channels"

Hyder Abbas and Syed Sair Ali are co-founders of Blink Co Technologies - which tailors its direct-ordering products for restaurants and other retail stores, enabling them to cater to their own quick-commerce customers.

Through Blink’s subscription-based service, restaurants can get online without paying a hefty commission to an aggregator, or spending on expensive technology development.
