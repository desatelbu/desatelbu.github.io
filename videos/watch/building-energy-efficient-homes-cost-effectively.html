The concluding video in APA’s four-part Performance Path Basics video series. Performance paths allow builders to value-engineer energy efficiency by viewing the entire home as a system, getting more bang for the buck by trading out high-cost building assemblies for less expensive, energy-efficient assemblies and systems elsewhere in the building. This video explains how builders can use APA’s Performance Path to Energy Code Compliance brochure to easily find cost-saving substitutions.
