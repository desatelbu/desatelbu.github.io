Armação dos Búzios (or Búzios), is a Brazilian resort located on an oceanic peninsula east of Rio de Janeiro. It is known as a sophisticated vacation destination with countless beaches. These include Ferradura, a quiet horseshoe-shaped bay with water sports, and Geribá, a popular surf spot. Between the streets of the village, the cobbled Rua das Pedras is a center for restaurants and nightlife.
