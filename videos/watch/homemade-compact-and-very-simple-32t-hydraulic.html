So I built hydraulic press used 32 tons hydraulic bottle jack and u profile metal. For plans - width 60cm (23.6in), height 80cm (31.4in), thickness 15cm (6in). Working space about 35cm (14in). In future I will need to upgrade springs, current is too soft.
It’s simple but very effective and by capacity is compact size.
