A Mangalorean Traditional Roce Ceremony of Beautiful Bride SONIA.
The Roce ceremony conducted one or two days before a wedding celebrates the last day of virginity of the bride and involves the parents’ blessings. Who is anointed with roce, a mixture of coconut milk and coconut oil.
