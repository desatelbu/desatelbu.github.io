The Lockheed Martin F-22 Raptor is an advanced stealth tactical fighter jet, exclusively used by the US Air Force. The F-22 Raptor is 18.9 m long, with a height of 5.1 m, wingspan of 13.6 m and has a range of more than 1600 nm. The aircraft is armed with a variant of the M61A2 Vulcan cannon internally above the right air intake.
