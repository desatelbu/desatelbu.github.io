In this 60-Second Gallery, feast your eyes on a series of still life paintings that recall exquisite meals from another era. Explore an assortment of fruits, fowl, and vegetables from German and Flemish masters including Abraham Brueghel, Joris van Son and Georg Flegel. These elegant pieces and more will be offered in Sotheby’s Old Masters Day sale (5 July London).
