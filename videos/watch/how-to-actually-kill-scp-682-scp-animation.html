SCP 682 is a Keter Class anomaly also known as Hard To Destroy Reptile.

This video is Part 2 of how you would kill SCP-682 based on our Community’s comments.

SCP682 is a large, vaguely reptile-like creature of unknown origin. It appears to be extremely intelligent, and was observed to engage in complex communication with SCP-079 during their limited time of exposure. SCP-682 appears to have a hatred of all life, which has been expressed in several interviews during containment
