Some of these clips feature moments where tourists get their boat charged by an angry hippo, two fishermen get attacked by a hippo, a brave russian man chases a hippo, kayakers have a close call with a hippo, two men in a car get chased by a hippo and a group of young fishermen get stalked by hippos.
