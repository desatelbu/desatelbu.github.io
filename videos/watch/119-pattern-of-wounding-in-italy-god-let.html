In Italy God let me get hurt in a big, deep way. Such that it was difficult to trust or have faith in God. Working through both my trust in God and the wounding has been SO hard. Is there a pattern to the wounding in your life? If so, INVITE JESUS IN. Say, “Jesus, come into this place. I give you permission to heal it.”
