It effortlessly ensures that things stay where you want them for as long as needed without sewing them on. Ideal if you’re looking for the satisfaction of a creative achievement – using the sandwich or pizza technique, for patchwork or when sewing trendy treats. Spray adhesive – not to be mistaken with a permanent glue – is colourless and odourless, easy to wash out and really practical.
