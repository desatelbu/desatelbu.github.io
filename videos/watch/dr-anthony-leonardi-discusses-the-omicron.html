In this video interview, immunologist Dr. Anthony Leonardi speaks with World Socialist Web Site writers Evan Blake and Benjamin Mateus about the Omicron variant and the COVID-19 pandemic. For more information and explanations of some of the scientific topics discussed in the video, read this two-part interview that Dr. Leonardi did last month with Benjamin Mateus.
