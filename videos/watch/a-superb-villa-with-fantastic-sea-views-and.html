The second floor hosts 2 large bedrooms with private en-suite bathrooms, both flowing onto a gorgeous south facing terrace with a jacuzzi looking out to sea. The stairway and first floor hallway boast a glass ceiling which provides a wonderful flow of light throughout the villa!
