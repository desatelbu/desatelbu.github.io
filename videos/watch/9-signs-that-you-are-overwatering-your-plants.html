Overwatering is a killer! Here are 9 signs that you are overwatering your plants.

1. Moldy Soil / Algae or fungus growing on surface
2. Yellowing Leaves
3. Brown, crispy dead Leaves
4. Soggy, mushy Leaves
5. Root Rot
6. Swampy Smell / Stinky Soil
7. Wilted Leaves
8. Plant not growing at all
9. Dead Plants
