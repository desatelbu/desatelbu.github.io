DAM! The Story of Kit the Beaver is an animated film that’s about not your average beaver! It’s the tale of Kit, a very independent (and easily distracted) young beaver who loves to explore the sounds of the forest she lives in.

DAM! The Story of Kit the Beaver was written and directed by Kjell Boersma and produced by Josh Clavir with the participation of Seneca College, School of Creative Arts and Animation, based on designs by Julie Flett. Music by Erica Procunier.
