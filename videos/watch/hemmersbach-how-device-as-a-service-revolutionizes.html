The IT industry develops highly innovative products. Running and servicing them has become increasingly complex for companies to manage in-house, with IT departments finding themselves under pressure as they try to keep up with the latest developments. Device as a Service (DaaS) is the answer. The global IT service provider Hemmersbach offers the state-of-the-art business model and revolutionizes how companies supply their employees with IT workplaces.
