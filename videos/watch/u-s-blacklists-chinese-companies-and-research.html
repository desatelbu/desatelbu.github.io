The United States added eight more Chinese companies, including the world’s largest commercial drone maker, to an investment blacklist this week. There are already around 60 Chinese firms on the list. The Chinese Foreign Ministry said on Wednesday that #China opposes U.S. new moves that overstretch national security and the unreasonable suppression of Chinese companies.
