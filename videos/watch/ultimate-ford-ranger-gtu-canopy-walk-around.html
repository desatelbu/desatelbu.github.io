This Ford Ranger build is a Swiss Army knife of builds! It has everything… even the kitchen sink! Jimmy takes us for a walk through of this ultimate Ford Ranger Canopy build.

At the heart of the GTU Model is the revolutionary Core Offroad kitchen design. Plenty of storage and lighting, two work benches, a removable sink, rebated sink tap with hot and cold water. All of this on a gas strut assisted soft open and close system.

A 110L upright fridge coupled with a 30L drawer fridge on the other side ensures there is plenty of storage for those long trips away. A 23 Zero 270 awning, 100L water tank, built in compressor, 120 ah Enerdrive Lithium Battery, Fusion stereo, central locking, internal STEDI RGB lighting and rear work lights and a Joolca hot water system. All of these features come standard with the GTU model.

On top of all this we have added an IKamper 4X roof top tent with 2x100watt solar panels and a rear mounted 23 Zero shower tent.

There is so much more to this setup and an absolute dream come true for our customer! Watch the video and tell us in the comments if we missed anything?
