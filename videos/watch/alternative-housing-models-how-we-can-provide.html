Some inputs on Alternative housing models from the summer school at Independent School for the City.

A quick look at models like: business as usual, erfpacht, HAT, Housing for all in USSR and Yugoslavia, Dutch social housing, Singapore Housing Development Board (HDB), Housing cooperations, collectives and syndicate, squatters and anti-squats.
