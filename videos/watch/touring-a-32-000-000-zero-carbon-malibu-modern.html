Located in the exclusive MariSol Malibu community, this modern ranch-style home is 100% electric, featuring Tesla batteries, solar panels, and electric car charging. The spacious chef’s kitchen is appointed with Miele appliances, Caesarstone countertops, and FSC-certified American white oak floors. Wellness elements include a Merv 13 Zehnder ventilation system to ensure clean fresh air while exercising in the gym or bathing in the free-standing tubs. Increase productivity with the Josh.ai private command system and speed through meetings in the Zoom room. Outdoor amenities include putting/chipping greens, a driving range, bocce ball and basketball courts. A saltwater pool and spa provide a tranquil retreat in the secluded backyard. Comfortable furnishings, curated paintings from local Los Angeles artists, and two years of estate maintenance are included. Don’t miss out on this captivating beach home.
