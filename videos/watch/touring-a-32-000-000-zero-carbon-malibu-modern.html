Listing Description: "This luxurious zero carbon home is set on two lushly landscaped acres, with over 460 feet of prime Pacific Ocean views. Located in the exclusive MariSol Malibu community, this modern ranch-style home is 100% electric, featuring Tesla batteries, solar panels, and electric car charging. Known as Zero 1, it is the first Zero Carbon Ready Certified home in California as designated by the ILFI, producing zero carbon emissions, operating or embodied. The spacious chef’s kitchen is appointed with Miele appliances, Caesarstone countertops, and FSC-certified American white oak floors. Adjacent to the kitchen is a Tuscan themed garden and outdoor dining pavilion set within a professionally maintained organic fruit orchard and vegetable/herb garden pollinated by on-site beehives. The 4,250-square-foot great hall is ideal for entertaining and exemplifies indoor/outdoor living with soaring 20-foot tall, FSC-certified Accoya ceilings, expansive skylights, a bar with a quartz waterfall countertop, and two refrigerated wine rooms. Wellness elements include a Merv 13 Zehnder ventilation system to ensure clean fresh air while exercising in the gym or bathing in the free-standing tubs. Increase productivity with the Josh.ai private command system and speed through meetings in the Zoom room. Outdoor amenities include putting/chipping greens, a driving range, bocce ball and basketball courts. A saltwater pool and spa provide a tranquil retreat in the secluded backyard. Comfortable furnishings, curated paintings from local Los Angeles artists, and two years of estate maintenance are included. Don’t miss out on this captivating beach home. Schedule a private tour today!"
