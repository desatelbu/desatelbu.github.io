Churchill Leadership Group is excited to talk with Coach Melanie, who will help us understand the importance of team bonding and how to incorporate a strengths-based approach.

In this webinar you will:

1. Understand why focusing on strengths is a great way to engage and bond your team and solve business problems.
2. Learn how to identify and harness individual and collective strengths.
3. Bust a myth – Learn how a strengths approach DOES NOT ignore weaknesses!
4. Become a proactive contributor of a powerhouse team.
5. Appreciate how a strengths focus can help you get more from your team.
