Antoine and Denzil from Ocean Conservation Namibia race ahead to catch a seal with a fishing hook in its mouth. The team need a few minutes to be able to remove the hook from his mouth, but the hook is finally out, and the seal is released back into the ocean.
This year we have seen more hook entanglements than the year before.
