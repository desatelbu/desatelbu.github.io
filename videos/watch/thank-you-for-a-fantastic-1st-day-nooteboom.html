On May 11th we kicked off the Nooteboom Trailers Transport Days 2017. We are honored to receive so many of our national and international relations involved in abnormal transport. We would love to see you at the 2nd day of this special event.

We also celebrated our our 135th anniversary and the fact that Dick Nooteboom has been with Nooteboom for 50 years. These are important milestones again in the history of Nooteboom.

- Take a factory tour with one of our tourguides
- Participate in workshops hosted by industry experts
- Chat with colleagues from the industry while enjoying a drink, snack and entertainment!

Also, for all our fans and other relations, we have a special ‘open day’ prepared for you this Saturday! Register here: https://www.nooteboom.com/open-day-13-may/registration-open-day-13-may-2017/

See you in Wijchen!
