And again, I enjoyed making this piece of jewelry. I McGyvered my way through this tutorial by just playing around a bit. And for me that is the beauty of metalsmithing. I hope I can inspire you to try and make jewelry like this yourself. There will be moments of failure, but eventually, if you don’t give up, they will lead to succes. In this tutorial I am showing you how you can make a tapered or cone shaped setting for a rosecut salt and pepper diamond. I am using a collet block to do so and am trying a new soldering technique which I would love to share with you.
