Soldiers from the Maneuver Battalion, Kosovo Force - KFOR, Regional Command - East, conducted training on a variety of weapons systems at the Kisic Range in Mitrovica.

Soldiers established defensive positions and practiced assaulting a trench line. U.S. and Latvian Soldiers shouldered the MK-19, Carl Gustaf, M2, GMG, Javelin, TOW/ITAS, and trained on claymore placement.
