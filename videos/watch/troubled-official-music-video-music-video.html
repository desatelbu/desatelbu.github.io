Heartstrings and Heartbeats are a global collective that explore mental health amongst creative people and their communities.

Using performing arts and sparking innovative conversations we search, co-create and implement solutions for mental health.

We wish to create a platform for creatives to speak their truths and share their thoughts through music, art, fashion and lifestyle.
