Displaying the song lyrics during worship is a great way to increase participation during the service, especially when people are not physically together. So you wanted to know - "Can we show the song lyrics when we stream our worship service?"



Yes. Your stream can have lyrics embedded in the stream itself or it can show lyrics in the background (for example, behind a song leader). Song Select makes this even easier with access to lyrics for songs covered by your CCLI Church Copyright License.




Just make sure to incorporate artist acknowledgements or writer credits for all songs used, and include following each song performed or at the end of the worship service.
