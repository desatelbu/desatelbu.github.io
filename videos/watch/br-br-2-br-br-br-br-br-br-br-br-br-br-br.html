The second episode of the Documentary Series The Yellow Shoes dives into the Subject of Faith.

What is Faith?
What is its connection with the Miracle?
How does it meet with Self-Consciousness?
Is there a space for it inside of us?
What is a Leap of Faith?

Without Faith What?
