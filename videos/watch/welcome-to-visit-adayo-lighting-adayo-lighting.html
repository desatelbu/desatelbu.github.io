ADAYO lighting is mainly committed to the development and production of LED smart lamps, LED home lighting, LED commercial lighting. Focus on Europe, South America, southeast Asia and other market . Establish a long-term relations and cooperation with international famous brands,such as Phillips/Ledvance/Havells/ Sylvania and so on.

The main modes of cooperation are OEM, ODM and SKD.
