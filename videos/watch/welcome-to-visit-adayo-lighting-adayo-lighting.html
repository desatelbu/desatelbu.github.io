ADAYO lighting is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Foryou Group (Founded in 1993, listed in Shenzhen Stock Exchange in October 2017, stock code: 002906). Foryou industrial park covers an area of 530,000 m².

ADAYO lighting entered the LED packaging industry in 2006, and established Huizhou Foryou Optoelectronic Technology Co., Ltd in 2012.Registered capital of 32 million US dollar. There are three business line: LED lamps, LED packaging and LED big power supply.

ADAYO lighting is mainly committed to the development and production of LED smart lamps, LED home lighting, LED commercial lighting. Focus on Europe, South America, southeast Asia and other market . Establish a long-term relations and cooperation with international famous brands,such as Phillips/Ledvance/Havells/ Sylvania and so on.

The main modes of cooperation are OEM, ODM and SKD. Professional sales team of 20 people , we can provide consulting sales services for different markets; R&D team has 60 people, one third of them with more than 20 years of experience in the lighting industry. Launched one new project or new product every month on average. The quality team has 15 people, control quality with industry standard from the incoming materials, production, and finished products ,provide strong quality support for your sales.

There are more than 50 production lines of packaging, power supply and lamps, about 300 workers, and 30 automatic and semi-automatic production lines, daily output of 100,000 led lamps.
