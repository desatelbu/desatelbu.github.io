I’m out here on Lake Erie on a misty, rainy day. I’m running the Stingnose Jigging Spoon, 3/4oz size. I went with the gold because of the cloudy weather, since gold reflects better on cloudy days. I’m drifting today, and slowly drifting and jigging off the bottom as we hook in a nice variety of fish!

Even that jumbo perch I was hoping for.
