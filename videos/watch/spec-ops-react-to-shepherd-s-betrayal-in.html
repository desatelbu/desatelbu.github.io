The Spec Ops guys are back reacting to potentially the most brutal deception in Call Of Duty history: Shepherd’s Betrayal! Call of Duty MW2 Campaign Remastered was released in 2020 and gave a fresh new life to possibly one of the best campaigns in COD’s history. Cameron and Israel walk us through some objectives from the game demonstrating their military knowledge and training as always to help identify realism and differences in the game to in the field. Make sure you have tissues ready for the end, you’re gonna need them! Let us know what you thought of today’s video in the comments below.

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 Campaign Remastered, a visually updated version of the original, was released for PlayStation 4 on March 31, 2020, and for Xbox One and Microsoft Windows on April 30, 2020. It only includes the campaign mode with no multiplayer and Spec Ops components; when purchased players unlock various cosmetic items in 2019’s Call of Duty: Modern Warfare and 2020’s Call of Duty: Warzone.
