Teochew Braised Duck Rice
Ingredients A：
500g of duck breast
1 pork intestine
2 tau kwa (firm tofu)
4 hard boiled chicken eggs

Ingredients B - Fresh Herbs:
2 fresh coriander’s roots
2 tablespoons of chopped shallot
Few slices of ginger
1 tablespoon of chopped garlic

Ingredients C - Dried Herbs:
2 cinnamon stick
1 bay leaf
2 star anise
½ teaspoon of black peppercorn
4 cardamoms
3 cloves
1 black cardamom, cracked
1 teaspoon of five spice powder

Ingredients D:
1 tablespoon of rock sugar
1 tablespoon of fried shallot

Ingredients E (For Garnishing):
Chopped spring onion
Fried garlic

Instruction For Blanching Process:
1) Wash and clean the duck breast.
2) Rinse well on the pork intestine and soak it under the water mixed with white vinegar for about 15 minutes.
3) Prepare a wok, add a few slices of ginger and some rice cooking wine into the wok.
4) Put the cleaned duck breast and pork intestine into the wok, immerse them with enough amount of room temperature water
5) Heat up the wok, use high heat to boil them for about 2 to 3 minutes.

Stir-fry Process:
1) Using the La Gourmet pressure cooker to do the stir-fry.
1) Add some cooking oil into the pressure cooker, heat up the oil.
2) Put the Ingredients B into the pressure cooker and saute them till fragrant.
3) Put the Ingredients C and D into the pressure cooker, saute them till fragrant.
4) Add in at least 2L of hot water, make sure the water level is good enough to submerge all the ingredients.
5) Add in 3 to 4 tablespoons of light soy sauce.
6) Add in 4 to 5 tablespoons of dark soy sauce.
7) Add in 4 to 5 tablespoons of Shaoxing cooking wine.
8) Add in 1 tablespoon of fish sauce.
9) Add in ½ teaspoon of salt.

Pressure Cooking Process:
1) Put in the Ingredients A into the pressure cooker.
2) Close and lock the lid of pressure cooker.
3) Press the cancel button of “open lid” cooking.
4) Select the “CHICKEN” button to pressure cook for 15 minutes.
5) Release the steam, open the lid of pressure cooker, and take out the braised pork intestine.
6) Close the lid, select the “STEW” button to continue pressure cook for 25 minutes.
7) Release the steam, open the lid of pressure cooker, and take out the braised duck, braised tau kwa and the braised eggs.
8) Prepare a serving plate and place some sliced cucumbers on the plate.
9) Cut the braised pork intestine, braised duck, braised tau kwa and the braised eggs into mouth bite size and put them on the serving plate.
10) Pour some braised broth on top and garnish with some chopped spring onion and fried garlic.
11) Serve with steamed rice, stir-fried cabbage, soup and chili sauce.

Soup:
1) Prepare a pan and add in a bowl of chicken stock.
2) Season the chicken stock with a little bit of soy sauce.
3) Heat up the chicken stock.
4) Prepare a serving bowl aside.
5) Take out chicken stock from the pan and pour into the serving bowl.
6) Take out some braised broth from the pressure cooker and mix with the chicken stock.
7) Add in some chopped spring onion, fried garlic and few dashes of white pepper.
