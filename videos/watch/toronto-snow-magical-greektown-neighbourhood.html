Hey guys! The Toronto snowfall season is here! This Toronto 4k walking video was filmed on November 28 2021 in Toronto’s Greektown neighbourhood, starting off on Pape Ave near Pape Subway Station.

After checking some of Danforth Ave, and the shops and restaurants lining the street, I continued my walk around the area by heading northbound into some of the residential areas. I went north along Logan Ave and took a look around the houses and the mature trees that were covered in snow. It really was amazing to see the snow on the trees and structures.

I walked back down to Danforth Ave and continued west along the street until reaching Danforth Ave and Broadview Ave.

Thanks for watching!
