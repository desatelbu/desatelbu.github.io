Traditionally installed and uplifted flooring typically ends up in landfill due to adhesive contamination.

And then there’s the damaged sub-floor to rectify - meaning that it can take half a day of time and money to uplift and repair a floor.

But there is a better way!

IOBAC installed flooring takes a couple of minutes to uplift, leaves the sub-floor undamaged and the flooring can easily be reused or recycled.
