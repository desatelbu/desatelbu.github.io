INSPIRING SUNDAYS WITH BHANTE RATHANASARA

Sunday, 29 Nov 2020		 10 am SG Time (GMT+8)

Title: “Are You a Potential Stream-Enterer?” by Bhante Rathanasara.

Stream entry is the first stage of sainthood and is often taught as a goal for the practitioner. Have you ever wished you could attain stream-entry, or is this wish even attainable? In this talk, Bhante will elucidate what it entails. Come find out if you are a potential stream-enterer!
