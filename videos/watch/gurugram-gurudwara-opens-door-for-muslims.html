Shri Guru Singh Sabha Gurudwara in Gurugram, Haryana has offered space to Muslims for Namaz. The step taken by the Sikh community has set a new example of brotherhood. “It’s ‘Guru Ghar’, open for all communities with no discrimination. There shouldn’t be any politics here. Basement is now open for Muslim brothers who want to offer ‘Jumme ki Namaz’,” said Sherdil Singh Sidhu, president of Shri Guru Singh Sabha Gurudwara.
