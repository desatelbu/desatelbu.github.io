As someone who lead a number of technology ventures early in his career, no one knows how to adapt and meet the changing needs of the market better than Dave Kauppi, President of MidMarket Capital, Inc. When Dave sees an issue his business is facing, he does not hesitate to implement a solution and continually revamp his business model until the problem is solved. Why does Dave say you should never be afraid to replace your business model? Find out in this interview.
