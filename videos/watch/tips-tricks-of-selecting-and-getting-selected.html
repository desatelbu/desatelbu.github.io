Trying to get an internship at a law firm? Not sure about which area to practice even after securing good marks? Unsure if your CV and Cover letter is good enough?
Watch this detail oriented webinar to learn more. The speaker explains the basic crux of the Legal profession and relevant steps before applying to a law firm, How to avoid mistakes in CV, tips for the interviews & things to keep in mind. The speaker also mentions a holistic approach towards planning internships for law students according to their academic year.
