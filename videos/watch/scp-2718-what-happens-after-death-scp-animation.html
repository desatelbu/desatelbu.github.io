All personnel, regardless of clearance, are forbidden to expose themselves to this article under any circumstances. Do not tamper with this warning without DAMMERUNG clearance.
SCP-2718 disappears from the article list for a period of time, until reappearing under a new ID.
No other living person is currently aware that SCP2718, this process, or this authorization exists.
