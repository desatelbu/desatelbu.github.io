Touch a Cube Location - Fortnite

For one of the Dark Jonesy "The Oracle Speaks" challenges in Fortnite Battle Royale, you need to "Touch a Cube ".

There’s a few cubes around the map, but most of them move about. The easiest cube to touch is the golden one in the very middle of the map in the Convergence location. Simply land here and bounce on the Golden Cube to touch it and complete the challenge / quest.
