Mr. Srinivas Venkatram (Founder-Lead, Illumine) shares examples from his work to demonstrate how it is possible to apply and work out the ideas and frameworks of Swami Vivekananda and the impact it can have.

In this talk, he specifically takes Swami Vivekananda’s Vedantic axiom - "Every person has infinite potential within" - as a foundation and explores how we can rebuild education, institutions and communities from the point of view of ‘awakening potential’.

This talk was part of an event hosted by Ramakrishna Vedanta Centre, UK on the theme: "Shaping the future of humanity: what we can learn from Swami Vivekananda".

The event was held on 02, December 2018 at the Mahatma Gandhi Hall, YMCA Indian Student Hostel, UK.
