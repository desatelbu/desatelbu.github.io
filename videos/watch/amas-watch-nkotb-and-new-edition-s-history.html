New Kids on the Block and New Edition teamed up for the first time ever for an epic performance at the 2021 American Music Awards. NKOTB sang hits like ‘The Right Stuff,’ ‘Step By Step,’ ‘Please Don’t Go Girl’ and ‘Hangin’ Tough,’ while New Edition busted out some moves to ‘Candy Girl,’ ‘Mr. Telephone,’ ‘Is This the End,’ ‘Can You Stand the Rain’ and ‘If It Isn’t Love.’ The 49th Annual American Music Awards aired Sunday, Nov. 21, on ABC.
