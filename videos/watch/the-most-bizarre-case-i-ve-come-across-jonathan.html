Jonathan Luna was a 36 year old man from New York. He pursued a career in law and managed to become the Assistant U.S. Attorney federal prosecutor. Although he was from New York, Jonathan was hired to work on serious criminal cases in Baltimore.

At 11pm Jonathan was at home when he received a call and told wife he had to return to the office to attend to some things. He got the office but around11:38pm he left… he did not have very good vision, and he needed glasses to be able to drive but he left them behind on his desk. Along with his laptop and mobile phone and embarked on a 200 miles journey that didn’t make much sense…

His body was discovered shortly after 530 a.m. by a worker behind Sensenig and Weaver Well Drilling warehouse in a seriously disturbing state.
