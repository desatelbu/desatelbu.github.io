After being a full-time carer for his mum for 27 years, Iain was hit hard when she sadly passed away. However, learning has proved a lifeline for Iain and improving his digital skills has given him the chance to find the support he needs and re-engage with the world.

Iain was supported by Corinth Training at Swinton Gateway in Salford, as part of the Digital You programme delivered by Good Things Foundation and Salford City Council.

Find out more at www.goodthingsfoundation.org/digital-you.
