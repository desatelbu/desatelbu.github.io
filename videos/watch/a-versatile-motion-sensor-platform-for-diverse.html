"oblu" is a versatile opensource development board for implementing innovative ideas on indoor navigation and wearable sensing. Some of the application areas are indoor tracking systems, gaming, virtual reality, autonomous robotics, STEM education, healthcare etc. It is an excellent development platform to realize many other IoT applications and indoor location based services.

A low cost platform designed especially for STEM students, researchers, IoT entrepreneurs and hobbyists.

Visit for more information: https://www.oblu.io/
