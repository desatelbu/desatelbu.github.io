Nasal breathing keeps air in your lungs longer than mouth breathing, and provides up to 20% more oxygen in your bloodstream at any given time. This will increase blood flow throughout the body, lowers blood pressure, boosts immunity, fights cancers, helps with eye health, and helps the lungs absorb more oxygen.
