A short video explaining how to infer meaning in a text with a focus on how you would use this to answer Question 2 paper 2 of AQA GCSE English language.

Inference is a key reading skills that is required of nearly all English exams, it helps for the deeper understanding of a text.

There are questions and answers. It builds to a ten minutes (timed question) with a sample answer at the end.
