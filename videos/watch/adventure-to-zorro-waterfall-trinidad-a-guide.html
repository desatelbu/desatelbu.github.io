My name is Alan Charles (the Trini Surfer) and I am here to show you the beauty of my home country TRINIDAD and Tobago as well as the rest of the world on my TRAVELS.

The amazing Zorro waterfall is located in Zorro Trace just off the North Coast Road, between Maracas and Las Cuevas.

The trek takes visitors across small streams and through a forested area located in the foothills of the El Tucuche mountain.
