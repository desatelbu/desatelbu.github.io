A LOVER of all things Japanese subculture, Katie Castles’ style is best described as Decora (the overloading of accessories) and Cyber pop (pastel colours with an e girl edge). Describing their look as “almost performance art” , Katie is so used to dressing up it’s easy to forget they are at the grocery store rocking a pair of bunny ears until other shoppers start taking pictures. The non-binary influencer isn’t afraid to occasionally go wig or makeup free on their IG page - but what about transforming into a completely different look? Drawn to wearing cutesy dresses and pink wigs, how will they find a pared back monochrome look with minimal accessories?
