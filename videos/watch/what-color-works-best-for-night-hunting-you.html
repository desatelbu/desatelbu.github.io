But are we as hunters looking at it from a human’s perspective when e should be asking what does the coyote see? Looking for success hunting at night, our brains have openly drank the kool-aid and believed THIS color light is THE ONE! They taught us a coyote will not respond to any other color than THIS one, simply because of it’s color. BUT IS THAT TRUE AT ALL???
It’s human nature to experience success hunting at night and want to connect or attribute that success to a specific color light we were using at the time. Why is that???
