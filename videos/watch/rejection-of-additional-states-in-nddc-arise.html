The Conference Of President-Generals of Ethnic Nationalities of Niger Delta has raised an alarm over what it calls a deliberate attempt to smuggle some non - contiguous states into the Act establishing the #NigerDeltaDevelopmentCommission. In a communique issued in #Yenagoa, the body also rejected the non - inclusion of states of the Niger Delta in the six hundred and twenty-one billion Naira earmarked for roads to be undertaken by the #NigerianNationalPetroleumCorporation. ARISE Correspondent, Ovieteme George reports.
