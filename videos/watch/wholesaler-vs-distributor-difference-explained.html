Hi Friends, in this video we will learn about the difference between wholesalers and distributors.

Wholesalers - Wholesaling or distributing is the sale of goods or merchandise to retailers; to industrial, commercial, institutional or other professional business users; or to other wholesalers (wholesale businesses)

Distributors - An entity that buys noncompeting products or product lines, warehouses them, and resells them to retailers or direct to the end users or customers.
