In this Romans 3 teaching, Paul was inspired by the Holy Spirit to communicate the total depravity of humanity and the list of indictments against God. The apostle has strung together a tapestry of Old Testament scriptures, in this practice known as “pearl stringing" a common Jewish teaching technique. The pearls in this passage establish that all have sinned and the answer is repent of our sin.
