Every person’s skin tone is different. With the development of Real Tone in the Pixel Camera and Google Photos, Google is committing to building an equitable camera that sees and highlights all people and the nuances of different skin tones beautifully and authentically. Starting with Pixel 6, the world’s most inclusive smartphone camera,* we are working to ensure everyone is seen how they truly are.

In this ad, follow Pixel 6 Pro as it moves through the life and experiences of a father and son.
