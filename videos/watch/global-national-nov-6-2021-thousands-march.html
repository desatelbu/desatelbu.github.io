Protesters worldwide hit the streets on the Global Day of Action for Climate Justice. Mike Drolet reports on the criticism facing Canada halfway through COP26 in Glasgow, Scotland.

In Ontario, 2.75 million people are now eligible to get a third COVID-19 vaccine dose. Among them are those 70 and older, as well as those who received two doses of the AstraZeneca vaccine. Brittany Rosen reports on who’s rolling up their sleeves, and who will have to wait.

At least eight people are dead after a crowd rushed the stage at a Texas music festival during a show by rapper Travis Scott. Houston police say they have opened a criminal investigation, with both their homicide and narcotics units involved. Jennifer Johnson looks into the investigation of the shocking tragedy.

There’s plenty of "Help Wanted" signs hanging in businesses in Canada, but employers keep getting ghosted. Anne Gaviola explains what’s behind the growing trend.

And, a run to remember. Rumina Daya has the story of a B.C. man who’s running 100 kilometres to raise funds for veterans’ mental health services.
