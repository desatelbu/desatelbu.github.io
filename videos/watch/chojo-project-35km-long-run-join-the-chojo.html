Join the Chojo Project’s team of sub 2:10 marathoners on this beautiful long run just outside of Iten. With a net gain of 350m and 700m of total climbing, this run was to get in milage, build leg strength and increase stamina.
Peter Bii talks us through his current team, speaks on selection and scouting, and shows off the beautiful surroundings.
