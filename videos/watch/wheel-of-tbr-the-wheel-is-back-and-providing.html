✨Rules:
I have changed the rules up for this year as follows:
Rule 1. Spin the wheel six times selecting six books to read based on the reading prompts.
Rule 2. Add an extra spin if the wheels stops on the same colour more than once.
Rule 3. Any books I don’t read I have to roll over to the next month.
Rule 4. I can’t buy any new books unless I completed (read) all of the previous month’s tbr picks.
