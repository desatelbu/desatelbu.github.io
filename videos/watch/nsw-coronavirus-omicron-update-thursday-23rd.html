NSW COVID-19: "Approximately 80% of our cases are currently Omicron. The increase in Omicron largely explains the steep rise in cases…the issue is the increased transmissibility, leading to high case numbers" - NSW Health Chief Health Officer Dr Kerry Chant.

5,715 new COVID-19 cases and 1 death were reported in NSW.
