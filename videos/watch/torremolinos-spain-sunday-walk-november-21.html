Torremolinos is a municipality in the province of Malaga that in recent years has become one of the preferred locations on the Costa del Sol. Apart from enjoying the sea and the sand, travelers have the opportunity to visit the fishing district of "La Carihuela" and taste its local cuisine, which includes fried fish, the most famous dish in the region, in hundreds of restaurants and bars.
