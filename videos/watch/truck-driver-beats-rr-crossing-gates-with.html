By now everyone knows what a DPU is and what is meant by "knocking down the signal". Right? Okay, well a DPU is a distributed power unit that is controlled by the engineer in the leading units and the DPU locomotives can be mid train, shoving on the rear or anywhere. When a train knocks down the signal, it means that the signal went from green to red as the train went past it. Back to the first train. The train was southbound heading toward Cincinnati.
