THERE IS A GIANT PHILIPPINES COLISEUM BEING BUILT IN SORSOGON BICOL… AND I HAD NO IDEA!

And that is where I get surprised by a massive coliseum! It is huge. I believe it may be a sports complex in the making? Anyways, from there, we find a cool little cafe, and head up towards Legazpi City.

Driving across the Philippines… always a unique experience!
