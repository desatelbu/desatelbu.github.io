THERE IS A GIANT PHILIPPINES COLISEUM BEING BUILT IN SORSOGON BICOL… AND I HAD NO IDEA!

We are headed to Catanduanes. On this day, we take the boat across to Matnog from Allen Northern Samar. After eating and sleeping at a pension house… we drive up to Sorsogon. And that is where I get surprised by a massive coliseum! It is huge. I believe it may be a sports complex in the making? Anyways, from there, we find a cool little cafe, and head up towards Legazpi City.

Driving across the Philippines… always a unique experience!
