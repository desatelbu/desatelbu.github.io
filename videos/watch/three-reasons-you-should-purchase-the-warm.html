Today I share Three reasons you should purchase the Warm Audio WA-87. It’s a replica of the classic Neumann U-87 mic. Truth be told, I’m not a big fan of the U-87 and have owned a couple of TLM-103 mics in the past only to sell them. My all time favorite is the U-47. Only one thing, I can’t afford one. Ha Ha! What lead me to purchase the WA-87 were the specs, the finish and the price vs performance. It is important to understand the value the Warm Audio WA-87 gives the amateur and professional alike. The fact that you get a microphone that so closely replicates the original is a plus. The $600 price tag is a bonus. You could hold out and spend $3000 for the original or for 1/5 the cost purchase this mic. If you decide to, then make sure you pair it with a quality mic preamp like the Warm Audio WA-73 or something similar. You are sure to find this mic versatile enough to mic everything from vocals to instruments. If you like this content please like, share, and subscribe. Thanks for watching;-)
