A TATTOO artist in Germany has spent $80,000 covering herself in superhero tattoos - transforming herself into a walking comic book. Juliane Forster, 33, from Oberndorf, Germany, decided to get her incredible tatts of Marvel and DC characters including Thor, Spider-Man, Captain America and Deadpool after bonding with her oldest son over their shared love of superhero movies. Living in a rural, conservative village with her husband and two sons, Julianne initially got a frosty reception due to her body art - but locals have warmed to her look, and now even come to her to get their own tattoos. With over 300k followers on Instagram, Juliane has become a big name in the world of tattooing and now says she cannot imagine life without them.
