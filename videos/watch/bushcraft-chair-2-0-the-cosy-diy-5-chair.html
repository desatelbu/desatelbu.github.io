In this video i introduce you my Bushcraft-Chair-2.0. Because the crossbar is not fixed to the front legs, this chair is completely foldable and transportable. The crossbar is only inserted into a paracord loop that is attached on the front legs with a brusik knot. This means that the seat height is also freely adjustable.
