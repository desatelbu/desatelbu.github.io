Tampa police have launched a death investigation after a second human body part was found in McKay Bay. On Thursday, a fisherman discovered what was confirmed to be a human leg. Friday, FOX 13’s Jordan Bowen was in the area where the leg was found when another fisherman approached and said he may have also found a body part. Now police say they’re trying to put the pieces together to identify who the limbs belong to.
