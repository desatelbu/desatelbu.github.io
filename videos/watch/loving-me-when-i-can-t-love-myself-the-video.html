The video is kinda pink indeed. The filmmaking was conducted during the months of July and August of 2021 and they depict various places in Greece. The storytelling of the video, as well as the poem, is a bit abstract because I feel the concept of love and mental health can be accurately depicted the less accurate you try to be. These are my thoughts and some glimpses of my summer, I hope you like it and maybe connect with it.

it was the sound of your voice
and the way it seemed you were hiding something

While you were sleeping I noticed your anxious breathing
but I didn’t say anything

when they can’t love themselves
Is accepting your inability to always make them happy

but I chose to ignore it

when they can’t love themselves
Is letting them find happiness
in the absence of you

more forced than the last

when you can’t love yourself

that this is not affecting me

The hardest part of loving you
when you can’t love me
Is hiding from you
that something is bothering me

but you didn’t say anything

but you chose to ignore it

when I can’t love myself
Is knowing that
I would do the same for you
