The storytelling of the video, as well as the poem, is a bit abstract because I feel the concept of love and mental health can be accurately depicted the less accurate you try to be. These are my thoughts and some glimpses of my summer, I hope you like it and maybe connect with it.

it was the sound of your voice
and the way it seemed you were hiding something

when you can’t love me

that something is bothering me

but you didn’t say anything

but you chose to ignore it

when I can’t love myself
Is knowing that
I would do the same for you
