When analysing Net Night Flows and thus leakage volumes it is essential to have an accurate Night-Day Factor, to ensure the leakage volume at night is extrapolated across a full 24 hour period. This Webinar will look at combining, measured large night users and samples of consumers night users with a measured N1 factor to get a more realistic Night-Day Factor for NRW assessment.
