How to make real beauty out of a regular glass vase. DIY decor. They took an ordinary glass vase for $ 3 and made it real beauty. DIY decor is a very exciting and interesting activity. Initially, the vase was completely unremarkable until we added some rhinestones to it, which brought it to life.

For decoration we used:
• Various rhinestones and beads;
• Polymer glue;
• Marker, thick thread;
• Nail polish remover;
