Rechargeable battery clinch and pierce unit ULBG 452 for mobile use inside and outside a factory.

The new BTM clinch and pierce unit ULBG 452 is characterized by its compact, ergonomic construction principle and its high joining force. In addition to efficient Li-Ion rechargeable batteries the unit is also optionally available with a power supply for all common voltage systems. Due to its low handling weight, the slim construction and the fast piston speed operating in narrow areas is well possible.

The stroke is activated by one push button, when reaching the front position retract stroke will be initiated automatically. The procedure can be interrupted at any time.
