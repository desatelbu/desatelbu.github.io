Right now, there is a large inventory of individual sewage systems in our municipality.
Therefore, many changes are now being made here in our area.
This is one of many upcoming projects that are about sewer systems, just so you know;)
I will try to make the videos as varied as possible.

This system is very environmentally friendly.
The plant does not need to be emptied, all waste is broken down by micro-life and plants and the clean water goes back into the ground.
The last tank is a phosphorus trap, in it it’s a bag that has to be exchanged, but I do not know the intervals.
