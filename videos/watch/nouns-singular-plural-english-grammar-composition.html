Nouns: Singular & Plural English Grammar & Composition Grade 2 Periwinkle

A noun can refer to one or more than one person, place animal or thing.
Singular Nouns: A noun that names one person, animal, place or thing is called singular noun.
Plural Nouns: A noun that names more than one person, animal, place or thing is called plural noun.
