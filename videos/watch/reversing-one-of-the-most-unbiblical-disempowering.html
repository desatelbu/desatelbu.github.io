In this video podcast, Pete Scazzero of Emotionally Healthy Discipleship exposes some of the most unbiblical language we use and how we can better communicate good theology so our people can flourish in their everyday lives.

It’s been said, “Fish don’t know they’re in water.” In the same way, it’s easy for us to become so used to “the way things are” in the church. Most of us, especially pastors and church leaders, don’t realize that the way we speak about work, calling, and ministry is often disempowering to others.
How does language affect our theology and create culture? Listen to this podcast now.
