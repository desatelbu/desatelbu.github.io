You can learn what it takes to have TRULY comfortable shoes by thinking about pushups!

How?

Think about this: when you do a pushup, do you squeeze your fingers together?

Of course not.

You spread them apart for stability, balance, and force production… which can help you get stronger.

Well, your feet are no different.

Your toes aren’t supposed to be bunched up and squeezed, but most shoes have POINTY toe boxes that don’t let your toes move naturally… so it’s more like doing a pushup with your fingers squeezed together.

- Natural Motion – super flexible to let your feet bend and move naturally. Low-to-the-ground for balance and agility.
- Natural Feel – the patented FeelTrue® soles give you just-right protection while still giving the ground feedback your brain needs for efficient, natural movement.
