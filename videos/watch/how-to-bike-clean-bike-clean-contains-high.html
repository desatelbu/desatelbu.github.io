Bike Clean contains high-quality surfactants and natural citrus oils designed to dissolve and breakdown the toughest dirt and grime.
Bike Clean is biodegradable and has been specifically formulated to provide effective cleaning without damaging or discolouring surfaces.

Biodegradable, ebike Safe, fast acting

Application
1. Shake bottle well before use.
2. Before applying Bike Clean, rinse surface to remove loose dirt and grime.
3. Spray directly onto all surfaces and allow to dwell for 3-5 minutes. In extreme cases, agitate the surface with a Bike Detailing Brush.
4. Rinse residue thoroughly and dry surface with a Bike Drying Towel.
5. Store bottle in a cool, dry place.
