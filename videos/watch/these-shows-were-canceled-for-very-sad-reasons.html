Every TV show eventually ends. Really successful ones often end on the terms of the cast and creators. Most get cancelled by the network after a drop in viewership, network politics, or because they’ve simply run their course. But in some cases, other circumstances force a TV series to end production.

Maybe one of the cast members has committed a crime or behaved badly in other ways. Maybe world events prevent production. Perhaps the network is worried about political ramifications. Or perhaps a beloved cast member passed away. All those reasons and more can be found here, in our list of shows that were canceled for very sad reasons.
