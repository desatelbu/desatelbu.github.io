The day has come. Second volume of our annual compilation finally sees the light!

‘Second Breath’ comes with 40 relevant tracks, all to be released on the next 3 weeks. It starts with the publication of the first part today, including 10 banging titles from talented artists such as Cleric, Draugr, Gijensu, Hadone, Inhalt der Nacht and Echoes Of October, JoeFarr, Julian Muller, NN, Raär and VII Circle.

Once again, we’ve appealed to the talent of K / Black Dog Bastard who did a perfect job for the artwork, using pointillism style to draw some mind blowing details.

We really wants to thanks again all artists involved in this massive project!

All parts will be available for free on our bandcamp, as well for the streaming on our soundcloud, and trough Hate channel for youtube.
