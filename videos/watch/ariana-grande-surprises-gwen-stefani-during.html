Ariana Grande stunned Gwen Stefani by attending the No Doubt singer’s final performance in her Las Vegas residency. The ‘Thank U, Next’ singer shared moments from the concert to her Instagram story on Saturday. Stefani later reposted Ariana’s footage and commented, ‘ummmm @arianagrande u came to my show?! yay love u thank you.’ One of the clips Ariana shared was of her fellow coach and competitor on ‘The Voice,’ Blake Shelton, after he joined his wife onstage to sing their duet, ‘Happy Anywhere.’ Plus, John Legend and Chrissy Teigen were also part of the star-studded crowd. The ‘Cravings’ cookbook author later praised Stefani on Instagram, gushing, ‘What a BEAAAAAUTIFUL show, Gwen!!! Congrats on an incredible run!!’
