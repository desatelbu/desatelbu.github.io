#Arbitrary #mywordbook

Meaning of Arbitrary: not done for any particular reason; chosen or done at random

Examples:
1. In spite of the cold weather, we made an arbitrary trip to the beach.
2. He made an arbitrary decision to take the car.
3. Although the rule was arbitrary and ridiculous, Jack was punished for breaking it.
4. Fortunately, income taxes are not arbitrary.
5. Emily made an arbitrary decision to drive across the country.
