Tony Trapani and his wife were married for more than 50 years. So when she passed, Tony was devastated and had to adjust to life after half a century of spending every day with the woman he loved.

After she died, Tony went to her filing cabinet and began clearing out her things. While going through her possessions, Tony came across something that he never would have expected.
