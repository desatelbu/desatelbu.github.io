Emergency hacks for all occasions



Hello everyone! Today we wanted to share some essential survival tips and hacks that one day may save your life (or at least help you feel comfortable in some situations).

First of all, we will show you a bunch of useful emergency hacks for parents. For example, if your kids get a slight injury while playing at the playground, you can use a tampon to stop the blood. If it happens at home, you can freeze a pack of marshmallows or other snacks and use it to reduce the bruise.

When you wear new shoes, usually, after a couple of hours, you can’t even take a step cause your feet are hurting. Prevent blisters on your feet with bra pads. With them, you won’t experience any shoes rubbing anymore ;)

If you saw a splinter under your skin, you can easily remove it with this clever hack. First, cover the splinter and skin around it with a liquid black mask. Let it dry, and then you can easily take it out! Another simple way to solve this problem is to use a syringe!
