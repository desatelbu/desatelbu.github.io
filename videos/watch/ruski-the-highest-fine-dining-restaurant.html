Ruski is the highest fine-dining restaurant in the world with Russian cuisine and breathtaking views over Moscow City. This restaurant with a panoramic view occupies the 85th floor of the "OKO" skyscraper and it seems to be floating in the air — the space is bright and breathing.

The elegant and modern atmosphere of Ruski perfectly corresponds to the spirit of the restaurant, offering its guests traditional Russian cuisine from chef Alexander Volkov-Medvedev. Check out the full tasting menu and vodka tasting in the ice bar.
