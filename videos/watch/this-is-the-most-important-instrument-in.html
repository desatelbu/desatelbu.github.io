Many instruments have defined many musical movements over the years, but theres one thats played a pivotal role in almost every one: The human voice. The way a singer uses their voice is one of the clearest ways of shaping a musical identity. Despite its clear significance, though, or perhaps because of it, the voice is notoriously hard to analyze. Theres so many factors that its hard to know where to begin, but hey, lets give it a shot.
