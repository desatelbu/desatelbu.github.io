13/11/2021
#USA
Several tornadoes touched down across #Connecticut and #RhodeIsland, toppling trees, downing power lines, and ripping the roof off a building in #NassauCounty
The first EF-0 twister carried winds of 65 to 85 mph when it touched down in Woodmere shortly after 2:30 p.m., then swept through #Hempstead, #Uniondale, and #Levittown
Significant damage was also noted near #CloverLane in Levittown, where a large tree was felled onto a house
No casualties were reported despite the property damage that knocked out power to more than 12,000 PSEG customers on Long Island

A waterspout occurred off the coast near #OceanGrove, #NewJersey

#Lebanon
Large fires broke out in #Tyre, consuming vast areas of forests and green spaces and threatening homes and residential neighborhoods
Firefighters are battling the danger of fires expanding from one region to another

#Egypt
Violent storms hit Egypt’s southern city of #Aswan, causing floods and triggering scorpion infestation that left three people dead and more than 500 others hospitalized due to scorpion stings
The storm brought heavy rain, hail, and dust storms to the city, forcing hordes of venomous scorpions out of their burrows and into streets and homes

14/11/2021
#Iran
Two shallow earthquakes registered by the USGS as M6.0 and M6.4 hit southern Iran’s #Hormozgan Province at 12:07 and 12:08 UTC (15:37 and 15:38 LT)
The agency is reporting depths of 5.6 km (3.5 miles) and 10 km (6.2 miles), respectively
The epicenters of both earthquakes were located about 63 km (39 miles) NNW of #BandarAbbas
At least one person is reported to have died and at least 27 people were injured

#Italy
Italian firefighters rescued several people isolated by flooding in #Sardinia during storms that forced the closure of schools and public buildings in #Cagliari
An 81-year-old man died in his car in Porto Pino after being swept away by water and mud

#SouthAfrica
Windows were shattered, and vehicles were damaged when golf-sized and even larger-sized hail pelted down during a severe hailstorm across #Lydenburg, #ThabaChweu Local Municipality
Some of the hailstones were as large as cricket balls and apples, most of them resembled the size of golf balls

#Mexico
After the heavy rains, which were accompanied by hail, different parts of the city of #Puebla were covered in white, mainly streets of the Historic Center of Puebla

15/11/2021
#Indonesia
Floods due to sea tides and exacerbated by moderate-intensity rain for the past few days have occurred in #Degayu, #Pekalongan, Central #Java
A total of 570 houses in Degayu Village were affected by tidal flooding with a height of between 30-50 cm (11.8 - 19.6 inches)

#Honduras
The heavy rains that have affected the city of #SanPedroSula, #Cortés Department, and its surroundings have caused severe flooding and landslides in the industrial capital and other areas of the Sula Valley

#USA
Heavy rain and high winds prompted extensive flooding and mudslides, forcing evacuations, school and road closures and a state of emergency declared in 14 Western #Washington counties
The Pineapple Express, also known as the atmospheric river, also brought wind gusts of up to 60 miles per hour
More than 158,0000 customers were without power in Western Washington

#Canada
The same atmospheric river caused major flooding and mud and rock slides in southern #BritishColumbia
Heavy rain battered the city of #Merritt, forcing the evacuation of an entire town, washing out major highways, and leaving many communities cut off from the rest of the province
