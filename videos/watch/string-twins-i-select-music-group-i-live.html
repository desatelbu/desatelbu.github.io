String Twins are a British string duo, made up of professionally trained musicians and identical twin sisters who studied violin and viola at The Royal Academy of Music in London.

Blending a fresh twist of contemporary Pop and House music with their unique virtuosic playing style, the pair have excelled in the live music world playing with some of the world’s most successful acts such as Adele, Ariana Grande, Muse, Beyoncé, Calvin Harris, Pink, Rita Ora and Noel Gallagher to name but a few.
