Soraya runs a weekly free global Satsang based on Nonduality. She uses the term ‘separate-self’. This term refers to the conditioned self made up of personal history which by its very condition is separate to the infinite all-pervading sense of being…aliveness…field of peaceful presence…consciousness. The I Am… without separation.
