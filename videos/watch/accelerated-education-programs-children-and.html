Children and youth frequently miss out on substantial amounts of schooling due to poverty, marginalization, conflict, and crisis. Responding to the needs of these learners has increasingly led governments and agencies to provide more flexible forms of education such as "accelerated education".

Accelerated Education (AE) is flexible, age-appropriate programming that promotes access to education in an accelerated timeframe for disadvantaged groups.
