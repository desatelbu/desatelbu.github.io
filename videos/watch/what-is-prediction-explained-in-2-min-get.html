n this video, we will explore What is Prediction.
Today, I predict that you’ll know what this word means by the time you finish listening to my video.

A prediction is a general statement about a future event that may be based on one’s experiences or knowledge. Simply put, it’s what someone thinks will happen in the future.
It’s impossible to guarantee accurate information about the future but one can always have a guess or estimation of what the future scenarios may be.
That way, you can surface out options and plan out possible developments and adjustments as you go.
Prediction entails different things in different settings. It is practised in many different industries all the time.
Some may be based on scientific calculations but most are just guesses and opinions.
For example, weather forecasting is based on science and technology that is used to predict the future conditions of the atmosphere.
While you might give your best guess in prediction when you have to let your friend know about your weekend plans.
Prediction encourages one to actively think ahead and ask questions based on what they have already heard, read, or seen.
It can even improve one’s critical thinking and problem-solving skills as you process and try to make connections on what you already know.
While this may seem trivial, it’s extremely useful on a grander scale like in business and medicine wherein prediction is crucial to success.
It can help you rule out possible problems and confidently move forward as you’ve prepared ahead of time.
While many situations in our life never fail to remind us how the future is always uncertain, it doesn’t hurt to try to predict what the future holds for us.
