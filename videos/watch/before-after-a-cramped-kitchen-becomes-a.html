See how skylights, hidden storage and a breezy color palette transform this kitchen! Designer Linda Mazur wanted to create a bright and welcoming space for the homeowners, so she added large windows looking out onto a spectacular backyard view. Greige cabinets, rich wooden accents and a spacious island are warm and inviting — perfect for guests and family to enjoy their breakfast. The quartz countertops and backsplash add subtle sparkle with natural light washing in from the skylights and windows. Watch the video to take a tour!
