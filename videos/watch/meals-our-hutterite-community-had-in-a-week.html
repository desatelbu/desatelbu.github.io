filling:
2 tbsp butter
1/4 cup onions
2 cups dry cottage cheese
1 egg
1 1/2 cup bread crumbs
2 tbsp sour cream
2 tbsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt
3 tbsp lemon
saute onions in butter, combine all ingredients until they hold together, make perogies add 2 tbsp filling into 3 inch rounds pinch edges to seal.
fry in oil on 350 degrees until golden brown serve with syrup and cream.
