Traffic volumes in Limpopo increased substantially Friday night, but slowed down Saturday morning. This as travellers make their way to the province ahead of festive season holidays. A few accidents have also been reported.
I spoke to SABC news reporter Lutendo Bobodi on the N1 Road in Mokopane, Limpopo.
