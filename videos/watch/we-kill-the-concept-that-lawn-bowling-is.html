If you’re like me, you barely knew that lawn bowling exist, let alone played by people under 65 years old.
I was excited when Aaron Zangle, the US National Lawn Bowling champion, reached out to introduce lawn bowling to me because I wasn’t sure how it was played but was eager to give it a try.
Aaron gave me a tour of the Hermosa Beach Lawn Bowling Club and a short lesson on how to play. I’m hooked! It’s not an old persons sport.
