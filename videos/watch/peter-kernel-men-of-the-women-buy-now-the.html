On January 12th, 2018 we had the pleasure to perform “Men Of The Women” live for the first time at Confort Moderne in Poitiers, France. We kindly asked the audience to film the live show and send us the material. This is the result.

Filmed by Renaud, Jeanluc, Damien, Michael, Anne, Camille, Eric, Lisa, Guillaume, Flavio, Myriam, Olivier, Rodolphe, Simon, Sebastien, Thib, Anastasia, Sandrine, Marine, Jule, Anna, Marie, Laurent, Mathieu, Stephane and Pierre.

We warmly thank all of you for the precious footage.

Edited by Peter Kernel
