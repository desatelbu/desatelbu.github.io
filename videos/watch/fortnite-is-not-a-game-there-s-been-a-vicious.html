There’s been a vicious battle going on for over a year between Epic Games, the creators of Fortnite, and Apple, who are known for being well, Apple.

If you go to the app store today you’ll find millions of games – but you still won’t find Fortnite over a year after its removal– and now, you’re unlikely to for a very long time…
