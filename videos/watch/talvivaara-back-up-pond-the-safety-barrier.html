This last safety barrier is something they have not wanted to show to the media.
Filming tours on the scene have been arranged around the gypsum waste pond.

travel to downstread and ground waters. All the huge masses of soil and ground will turn into a toxic waste at the watershed area - by the source of Savolax and Kainuu waters.
