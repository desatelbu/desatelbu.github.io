Balsamic Chicken

Cook up some halved brussel sprouts in coconut oil. We added bacon crumbles near the end of them being soft. Cook chicken thighs OR breasts separately and shred the chicken. Add it to the pan with the brussel sprouts. For the sauce, we use about 1 1/2 cups of mayonnaise and half a cup of balsamic vinegar plus some basil and salt to taste. Add more balsamic vinegar in until it is just the right amount of tangy.

Next time I make this I will try to be more careful to measure things out for a more accurate recipe!
