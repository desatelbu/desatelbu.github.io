Animated sequence used for the inauguration of the New Acropolis Museum in Athens, 2010. Part of a 7-building video mapping project that surrounded the museum during its initial opening nights.

This short film, depicting the construction of the Parthenon, is based on the Sketches of archeologist Manolis Korres.

Video directed by Christopher Brellis and Athina Tsangari
3D modelling and render by Kostas Gkikas
Sound design / music by Stavros Gasparatos
