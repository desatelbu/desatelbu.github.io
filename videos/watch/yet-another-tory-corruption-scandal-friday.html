PLUS

- COP26, Professor Rupert Read joins us from Glasgow to discuss the ins and outs of the UN Climate Conference, including Greta Thunberg’s dramatic walk out to join protestors outside after she called the conference "Greenwashing".

- George Monbiot breaks down where our leaders are going wrong and what we can do about the environmental damage being done to the planet, including the recent sewage crisis affecting UK rivers and coastlines.
