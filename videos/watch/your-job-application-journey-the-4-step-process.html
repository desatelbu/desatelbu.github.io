Your Job Application Journey

=====
SUMMARY
=====

In this video, I will show you why you never hear back from an employer when you submit your resume.

Why?

Because job seekers often complain that potential employers more closely resemble black holes than functioning companies.

Below are the four process steps of your job application:

1. When you submit your resume to a company, you are submitting it to the Applicant Tracking System, not an actual human being.

2. Once your resume gets past the Applicant Tracking System, there are meetings behind closed doors between HR, hiring manager and his/her team.

3. If you are NOT selected, you might or might not get notified.

4. If selected, you will be contacted.
