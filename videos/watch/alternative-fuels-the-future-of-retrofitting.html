This webinar addresses the topic of retrofitting, exploring the practice as an opportunity for existing vessels to meet the latest legislation, improve GHG emission performance by burning the latest generation of zero emission fuels, and also remain attractive as a business asset. The webinar was hosted by the Getting to Zero Coalition’s Fuels & Technologies workstream as part of the workstream’s Insight Briefing Series.

Panelists include:
05:51 Amar Parikh - Retrofit Development Manager, Man Energy Solutions
14:45 Lampros Nikolopoulos - Projects & Dry Docking Engineer, Euronav
22:58 Harry Robertsson – Technical Director, Stena
29:47 Ulrik Frorup - Special Project & External Affairs Director, Bureau Veritas

34:30 Q&A

Moderated by Emma Skov Christiansen, Lead, Shipping Emissions & Ocean Agenda, World Economic Forum/Getting to Zero Coalition

#GettingtoZeroCoalition​
The Getting to Zero Coalition is an industry-led platform for collaboration that brings together leading stakeholders from across the maritime and fuels value chains with the financial sector and other committed to making commercially viable zero emission vessels a scalable reality by 2030. The Coalition is a partnership between the Global Maritime Forum, the Friends of Ocean Action, and the World Economic Forum.

Learn more at https://www.globalmaritimeforum.org/getting-to-zero-coalition
