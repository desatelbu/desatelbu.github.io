TLC Group is a Swedish-Polish producer of steel stairs, platforms and temporary protection systems for industry and building sector with a modern and automated production plant in Poland. The combination of Swedish technology with Polish enterprise allowed to specialise in selected group of products of the industry market. Thanks to that we can offer a worldwide complex sales and rental service including project, production and assembly of our products.

TLC has the certificate of conformity of Factory Production Control with EN 1090-1 issued by TÜV SÜD which guarantees the highest quality level of all TLC products. Some of our products are CE marked.
