BREAKING NEWS.
TURKEY. PAKISTAN. AFGHANISTAN. USA. RUSSIA.
A powerful explosion took place a few hours ago, in the largest mosque in the capital of Afghanistan, Kabul, bringing death to many civilians who were on the spot.
Although the information about the victims is still confused, what is reported is that at the moment, a farewell ceremony was taking place in the mosque for the mother of Zabihullah Mujahid, a representative of the Taliban, so everything converges that this is a possible attack by ISIS-K forces.
This, of course, gives an even better picture of what is happening right now in Afghanistan, where the informal civil war continues.
