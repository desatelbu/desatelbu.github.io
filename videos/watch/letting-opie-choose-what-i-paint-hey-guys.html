Hey guys! Remember that trend from like a year ago where everyone was letting their pets choose stuff? Like, "my dog pick my outfit" or "letting my cat choose my makeup." WELL. Of course I didn’t manage to jump onto that trend WHILE it was..ya know…TRENDING. BUT. I’m here now, awkwardly a year later and ready to give this a try 😂
SO, I decided to let my cat Opie choose my art supplies or pick out what I am painting in this video. I let him have several options including painting on squishies, painting a canvas, painting on a leather backpack and we shall see which one he picks. Hopefully you enjoy what I end up customizing and decorating today!
