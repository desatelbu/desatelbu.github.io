The central idea of Order addresses the necessity for predictability, or control over reality. While effort of such governance is most obvious through the routine lives of subjects regulated by certain authority, such reality also presents a brutally objective world in which anything, or anyone found at direct odds with what is accepted being circumscribed.

Panel 2: Content is a panel discussion that invites artists, especially practitioners that deal with moving images, to discuss the possibilities offered by the medium in representing or highlighting issues of Orders through film.
