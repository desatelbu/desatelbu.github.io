BREAKING NEWS.
SYRIA. TURKEY. RUSSIA. USA.
According to a report on the website of the Syrian Observatory for Human Rights, after the airstrikes carried out by Russian fighter jets against targets near Turkish bases, Turkey immediately sent reinforcements, thus further strengthening its positions in the region.
More specifically, sources of the Observatory report that ten trucks full of ammunition and weapons arrived at the Turkish positions, located in the province of Idlib, at the Taftnaz air base and at the al-Mastuma camp, thus sending a message to the Russian side.
At the same time, it is pointed out that at the moment when these reinforcements arrived, many movements are being made by the Turkish forces, in most of the observatories it has, within the de-escalation zone.
