The sixth installment of the Call of Duty series, Modern Warfare 2, was released by Activision in 2009. In the campaign of the game, players will travel around the world as part of Task Force 141 tracking Vladimir Makarov, leader of the Russian Ultranationalist party, and U.S. spies.
