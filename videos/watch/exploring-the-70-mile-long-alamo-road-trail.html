This is the first of two videos of my exploring and driving the 4WD dirt-surfaced Alamo road trail that runs for 70 miles from where Kenny Veach disappeared (Las Vegas, Nevada) out across the desert and near the secret military base Groom Lake and Area 51. The military testing base was heavily posted and I did not attempt to go cross country on foot for a closer look. Too many eyes in the sky for that.
I do find a lot of Indian stone tools and find some petroglyphs along with a dried-out but perfectly preserved desert tortoise shell, a really cool badger skull, and spent WW2 munitions.
