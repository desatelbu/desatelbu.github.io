With his fast finish on the flat and on inclines, Christophe Laporte has become a consistently high performer in races across Europe, taking a recent victory at the GP de Wallonie ahead of the likes of Warren Barguil and Patrick Konrad. We were lucky enough to take a look at his beautiful De Rosa Merak, which Laporte and the rest team Cofidis have been riding throughout the 2021 season.

Frame and forks: De Rosa Merak
Wheels: Fulcrum Speed 40T/55T
Tyres: Michelin Power Competition
Cockpit: Vision Metron 5D
Saddle: Selle Italia Flight Flow
Shifters: Campagnolo Super Record EPS 12s
Brakes: Campagnolo Super Record Disc
Front Mech: Campagnolo Super Record EPS 12s
Rear Mech: Campagnolo Super Record EPS 12s
Chainset: Campagnolo Super Record
Pedals: Look Keo Carbon Blade
Chainring size: 53/39
Powermeter: SRM
Cassette: Campagnolo Super Record 12s 11-29T

Bike weight: 7.7kg
Saddle height: 80cm
Reach: 59cm
Bar width: 42cm
Stem length: 130mm
Crank length: 172.5mm

Finishing touches: Elite Cannibal XC Bottle Cages
