This scary footage shows two boys playing at the very top of a 22-storey building in the Chinese city of Xianning. The rule is simple: to jump over a gap between the roofs of the top floor’s balconies.

Luckily, no one was injured: the witness who filmed the video said he called the local property management, who then sent staff to bring the boys down.

The roof door is now locked properly.
