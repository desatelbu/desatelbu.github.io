Scientists can explain most everything animals do, but there is still the occasional bizarre behavior of theirs that doesn’t have an official scientific explanation yet, despite many theories that have been presented. In fact, scientists have been confused by this for years, and that’s just one of the strange animal behaviors they don’t quite understand yet. From the mystery of shark navigation to those petty, petty crows, let’s take a look at some animal behaviors that still haven’t been explained by science.
