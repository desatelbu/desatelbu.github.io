If you want to find out more about Schecter Guitars at Andertons, then just click here - http://blog.andertons.co.uk/guitars/schecter-guitars-available-andertons

The amp used in this video was a Marshall JVM210C, but plugged in to a Celestion Vintage 30 loaded Victory 4x12.

Jammy Dodgers & general off camera abuse was courtesy of Adam, our Schecter rep (who is awesome by the way!!).
