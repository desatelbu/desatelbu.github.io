Another chilly start to the morning but temperatures are expected to warm-up nicely this afternoon. An area of high pressure will be anchored over region today and through the first half of the work week before a cold front moves through.



Models are a bit off on the timing of the front but showers are expected sometime on Thursday and perhaps through Friday morning. That front may also bring gusty winds but nothing damaging. When that front is entirely out of the area, highs are expected to be in the lower to middle 60s on Friday and upper 50s next weekend.
