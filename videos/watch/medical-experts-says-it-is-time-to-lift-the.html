The night curfew in Kenya is still in effect 18 months since the outbreak of the Covid-19 pandemic. with the current measures lapsing on October 19th, pressure is mounting to have the 10pm to 4am curfew lifted. Health expert and AMREF CEO Dr Gitahi Githinji says nationwide curfew helped during the response phase to the pandemic but it is now time to lift curfews.
