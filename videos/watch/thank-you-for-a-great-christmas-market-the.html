“La Campagne en Ville, The Christmas Market”

Thursday, December 16th to Sunday, December 19th from 12pm to 8pm.

A “Nature by Marc Beyrouthy”, “NoGarlicNoOnions” and “Atayeb El Rif” event.

This market is supported by Empower Lebanon, LRI, and Coral.

Highlights:

The event will be broadcast live on NoGarlicNoOnions YouTube Channel

NATURE team is available to answer any agriculture technical question you might have.

This special event is organized for you to be able to support people passing through hard times: Buy Abou Chadi Famous Hummus for a cause, give Mouneh for the families, shop your Christmas needs from families joining from the four corners of Lebanon. Contribute to the food bank, buy a “donate box” from (Made by Nature).

Amazing food experience

There will be live cooking stations where visitors will be able to taste traditional food from all four corners of Lebanon and more.

- The Christmas classics: Mulled wine (Vin Chaud), roasted chestnuts and corn on the cob, locally made sausages, candies, waffles, crepes, croissant, ice-cream, Saj and much more.

- Meet Candy Yaghi, the award winning foie gras queen!

- Taste Midos unique burgers and roast beef sandwiches.

- The one and only Potato bar is back!

- Abou Chadi the king of Hummus will be serving his famous Balila for a cause.

- The Rif Truck will be serving traditional Lebanese food & drinks freshly prepared by women from the villages from Rural Delights "Atayeb Al Rif" Cooperative.

110 Lebanese heroes

This market is our way of supporting Lebanese heroes and enabling them to connect with consumers. Come for the heroes, meet the creators, and stand beside fellow Lebanese who are pushing on to make it through these tough times.

Artisans, creators, women cooperatives, farmers, local producers, and more

From 2 till 92

Young and old, join us at “La campagne en Ville” come and enjoy daily :

Christmas activities for kids
Live music
Christmas caroling
Christmas parade

Christmas is here, let’s celebrate it in the best sustainable way possible.
