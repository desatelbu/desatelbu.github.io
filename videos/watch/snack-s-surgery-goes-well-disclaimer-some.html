Wow! Did you see the size of those teeth!!? Over 2 inches! Snack’s has a condition known as Malocclusion which causes teeth to grow out of control. If let untreated, the animal will die.

We can’t thank Rehabber Deb and Dr. Chris for all their hard work and amazing skills enough! They saved Snack’s life!
