Collaboration requires team members who are willing to share their experiences and skills and learn from one another. This means navigating a wide variety of approaches and work styles. These working styles can affect how people make decisions, deal with change, deal with risk, and deal with each other. Turning these diverse styles into an overall cohesive team can present a serious challenge.
