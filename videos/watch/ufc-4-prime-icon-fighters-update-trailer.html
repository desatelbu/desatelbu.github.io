The newest update to UFC 4 is live with an all-new fighter, updates to vanity, and new PRIME ICON fighters for a fresh UFC 4 experience.

PRIME ICON fighters are a new type of fighter that highlights some of the best athletes in the UFC.
Icons of UFC history return to the EA SPORTS UFC Octagon with the ratings and skills they had in their prime fighting years.

The first set of PRIME ICON fighters added include Khabib Nurmagomedov, Georges St-Pierre, Anderson Silva, Michael Bisping, Alistair Overeem, and Joanna Jędrzejczyk.

Also making his UFC 4 debut, Russian lightweight fighter Islam Makhachev is entering the Octagon!
