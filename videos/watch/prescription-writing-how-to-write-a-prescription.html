A medical prescription is an order (often in written form) issued by qualified health care professional (e.g. physician or dentist) to a pharmacist or other therapist for a treatment to be provided to the patient.

Prescription orders are divided into two types depending upon the availability of the prescribed medications.
(i) Pre-compounded and
(ii) Extemporaneous or Compounded Prescriptions
