The UK spends around £200 billion a year on welfare benefits. "On Benefits And Proud" explores the lives of people some of these people across Britain who are living on benefits and are proud to do so. We see 37-year-old Heather Frost labeled as the "Dole Queen" and her 11 kids who get £900 a week in benefit money from the government. In order to afford what she clams on benefits, she’d need a job paying £60,000 a year. We also see how single parents cope living on benefits and we meet Julie and Vinny who have been unemployed for over 6 years who play the benefits system to ensure they don’t have to work.
