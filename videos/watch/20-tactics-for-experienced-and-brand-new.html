20 tips for investors, experienced or brand new

Look into the success of previous roles of the executives at the company. That includes the chief executive officer, chief technology officer, and chief operations officer.

Stay on your investment course until you see ongoing success with your trades…

Do NOT look to overseas stocks, or different types of investments until you master the first type or strategy.

Take advantage of all the free investment tools and knowledge available for investing.

Conduct a fiscal audit, then build your investing plan.

Commit to learning about investing well (from your trades/ stock market books/ YT Investment videos…)
