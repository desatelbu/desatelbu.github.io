In this video I show you how you can easily decorate a simple notebook with stamps, embossing powders and colored pencils.

For this diy project I used the Altenew Persian Motifs Stamp Set, Faber Castell Pencils and the Hero Arts White Embossing Powder.

You will see how easy it is to color kraft paper with colored pencils and gamsol.
