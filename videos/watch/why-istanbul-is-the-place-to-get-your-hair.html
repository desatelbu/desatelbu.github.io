Every year hundreds of thousands of men pack their bags and fly to Turkey because they want their hair back. The nation’s hair treatment industry is worth north of $1 billion, but clinics are lightly regulated and technicians rather than doctors often do most of the work. As the sector expands, some wonder whether profit is being prioritized over patients.
