And lucky for them, there is no shortage of nail-bitingly scary videos on the internet to feast your eyes on! But before you go ahead and plunge headlong into this list of scary videos analysis, let me warn you: These hauntingly creepy videos are an absolute no-no if you are home alone. Trust me, they are freaky enough to stay with you long after you have watched them and will ruin your good night’s sleep for weeks to come.
