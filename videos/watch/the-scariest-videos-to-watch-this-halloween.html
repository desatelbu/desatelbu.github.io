And lucky for them, there is no shortage of nail-bitingly scary videos on the internet to feast your eyes on! Therefore if you find yourself all alone on a Friday night and craving something that will give you the most nerve-wracking of shivers — something that’s not already in your well-watched library of scary videos, here are a few horror gems you may not already have seen, but need to see to fire up your Halloween! But before you go ahead and plunge headlong into this list of scary videos analysis, let me warn you: These hauntingly creepy videos are an absolute no-no if you are home alone. If you are into scary stories, haunted house tales, and eerily sinister mysteries, then look no further than this scary comp. These scary videos are bone-chilling even for a horror veteran like me. Trust me, they are freaky enough to stay with you long after you have watched them and will ruin your good night’s sleep for weeks to come.
