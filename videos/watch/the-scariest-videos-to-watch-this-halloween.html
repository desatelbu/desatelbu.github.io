And lucky for them, there is no shortage of nail-bitingly scary videos on the internet to feast your eyes on! In fact, now’s the perfect time to jump in at the deep end of the scariest videos out there, and settle in for a night of fright.

Therefore if you find yourself all alone on a Friday night and craving something that will give you the most nerve-wracking of shivers — something that’s not already in your well-watched library of scary videos, here are a few horror gems you may not already have seen, but need to see to fire up your Halloween! But before you go ahead and plunge headlong into this list of scary videos analysis, let me warn you: These hauntingly creepy videos are an absolute no-no if you are home alone. If you are into scary stories, haunted house tales, and eerily sinister mysteries, then look no further than this scary comp. These scary videos are bone-chilling even for a horror veteran like me. Trust me, they are freaky enough to stay with you long after you have watched them and will ruin your good night’s sleep for weeks to come.

You may probably want to double check all the doors and windows in your home before you get too deep into this list of scary videos. That should help you calm a bit down and regain your senses. As a matter of fact, I use this advice, when I’m on the lookout for something truly horrifying — being conscious of the fact that nothing is happening to you in person helps you go through the most chilling of scares — think of it as a Halloween-special pro tip from yours truly.
