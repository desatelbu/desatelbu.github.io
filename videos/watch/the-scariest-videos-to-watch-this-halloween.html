But before you go ahead and plunge headlong into this list of scary videos analysis, let me warn you: These hauntingly creepy videos are an absolute no-no if you are home alone. If you are into scary stories, haunted house tales, and eerily sinister mysteries, then look no further than this scary comp. These scary videos are bone-chilling even for a horror veteran like me.
