Built for tanning salons to fit all sizes, our tanning software is packed with core features to help you automate and streamline your business. With robust automated, integrated billing, point of sale (POS), online sales, appointment booking system with online booking for customers, staff and customer management, and much more! Tan-Link has all you need to run your tanning salon.
