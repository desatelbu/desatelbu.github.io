I love to turn something super simple into something super fun and adorable. When I shared my first glitter ornament tutorial a few years ago, you all went nuts for it and I’ve seen everyone make so many glitter ornaments since then. I’m excited to revisit this and show you the simplest way to make easy DIY glitter ornaments … and this time, we’re adding cute animal faces and ears!
