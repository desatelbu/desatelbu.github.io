Ingredients:
pork neck - 700 g (24.69 oz)
mayonnaise - 40 g (1.4 oz)
balsamic sauce - 30 ml (1 fl oz)
ketchup - 30 g (1 oz)
TO MARINATE 30 MIN

olive oil - 30 ml (1 fl oz)
Tray size 22 x 26 cm (8.66 x 10.24 in)
