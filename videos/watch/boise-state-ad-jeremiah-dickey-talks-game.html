KTVB sports director Jay Tust got a chance to sit down with Boise State athletic director Jeremiah Dickey for an extended conversation about everything Boise State football and athletics.

Dickey, who is nearing a full year as Boise State’s director of athletics, discussed his vision for the Boise State football program and the university’s athletics as a whole, including a possible athlete’s village, renovation to Albertsons Stadium and more.

The video has been lightly edited but is largely the complete conversation between Tust and Dickey.
