WHAT!?? Jenny is going to disneyland without us!? NOOOO! we can’t let here go. grab her legs Adley, Niko block the front door, I’ll play hide n seek with her suit case! She is such a good mom and honestly a superhero for all that she does for our family that I couldn’t be happier she is going on a little vacation. That does mean that I am in charge of all three kids…by myself for the next few days!!

But Jenny being the amazing mom she is helps us get all ready for her being gone. She reminds me about Navey’s bedtime routine, that Adley needs to do her school work, and Niko Bear should eat food lol. We all hug and kiss goodbye. We are going to miss Mom and she is going to miss all of us. But she’ll have so much fun!

Adley has dance class so we hurry up and get her dressed and to her practice. When we are done, we head to the spacestation to check in on our next animation video. It looks like Adley might have sticker pox!?! We also we have sword battle on the slide, but its ok, they are just pretend swords. After the spacestation we stop at the grocery store to pick up supplies. We grab cereal (why wouldn’t we, we all love it) and coloring books, crayons, and art supplies because we love doing crafts! This is going to be fun.

Once we get home, We check in with Jenny, who is on an airplane. Then Niko and Adley color Bluey (like our family halloween costumes) and Paw Patrol while I put Navey in bed. We then end the night coloring as a family before bed time. Maybe this won’t be so tough after all…
