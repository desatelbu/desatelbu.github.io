But when her colleagues discovered her OnlyFans account, Allie Rae was forced by the hospital to leave the nursing career she loved. Now working as a full-time OnlyFans model, Allie Rae earns over $250,000 a month, giving her the financial freedom and flexibility to be with her husband and their three teenage sons. Despite the backlash she has received for her OnlyFans career, Allie Rae says she is happy with where she is. She told Truly: "I’m judged for being a mum and an OnlyFans model almost all the time.
