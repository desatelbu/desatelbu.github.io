Why is Agriculture Important and its Role in Everyday Life ?

For decades, agriculture has been associated with the production of essential food crops.

At present, agriculture above and beyond farming includes forestry, dairy, fruit cultivation, poultry, beekeeping, mushroom, arbitrary, etc. Today, processing, marketing, and distribution of crops and livestock products, etc. Are all acknowledged as part of current agriculture?

Thus, agriculture could be referred to as the production, processing, promotion, and distribution of agricultural products. Agriculture plays a critical role in the entire life of a given economy.

Agriculture is the backbone of the economic system of a given country.
