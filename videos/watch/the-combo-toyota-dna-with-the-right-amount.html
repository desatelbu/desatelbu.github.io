We feature another totally awesome full, body-off restoration of a 1978 FJ40. This FJ40 also received lots of our other popular additions including Dakota Digital instrumentation, our premium version of the Retro Audio sound system. Vintage Air AC, Rigid Lighting, leather seating, and much more. Check it out!
