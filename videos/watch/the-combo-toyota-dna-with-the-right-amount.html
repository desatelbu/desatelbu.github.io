We feature another totally awesome full, body-off restoration of a 1978 FJ40. This flawless Land Cruiser sports the very popular Cummins R2.8 coupled to a new Toyota H55F 5 speed transmission and 19 spline split transfer case. The PRLC shackle reversal and wheel base extension gives the classic 4X4 better drivability on and off road. This FJ40 also received lots of our other popular additions including Dakota Digital instrumentation, our premium version of the Retro Audio sound system. Vintage Air AC, Rigid Lighting, leather seating, and much more. Check it out!

Also, take another trip through the shop and learn about putting a Toyota 3FE and A44F automatic transmission in an old FJ40. Find out what NOT to do when mounting seats in this episodes "STOP IT" and more.
