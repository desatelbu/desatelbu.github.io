A huge tornado swept across Canada in the city of Vancouver. Many trees were broken, many cars and roofs were damaged, and significant material damage was caused. Power lines were also knocked down, which led to power outages in many areas of the city. The tornado passed near the city’s airport, which led to a temporary suspension of the airport and delays of some flights. There were no reports of casualties. The tornado was accompanied by rain, after which a rainbow of incredible beauty appeared in the sky over the city.
