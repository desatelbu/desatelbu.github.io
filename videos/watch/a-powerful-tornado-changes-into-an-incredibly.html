Power lines were also knocked down, which led to power outages in many areas of the city. The tornado passed near the city’s airport, which led to a temporary suspension of the airport and delays of some flights. There were no reports of casualties. The tornado was accompanied by rain, after which a rainbow of incredible beauty appeared in the sky over the city.
