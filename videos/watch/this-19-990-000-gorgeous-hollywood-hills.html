Presenting this immaculate Hollywood Hills contemporary promising the ultimate luxurious California living, perched above the famed Sunset Strip with jetliner views. Tucked away from the city, the home sits at the end of a private, gated street and is enveloped by explosive vistas from DTLA to the Pacific. With a sweeping open floor plan and endless indoor/outdoor flow, the compound is a true entertainer’s paradise.
