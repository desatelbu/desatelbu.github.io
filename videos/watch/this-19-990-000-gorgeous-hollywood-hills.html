✔️ ABOUT THE PROPERTY: 1 BODE DRIVE, LOS ANGELES, CA
Presenting this immaculate Hollywood Hills contemporary promising the ultimate luxurious California living, perched above the famed Sunset Strip with jetliner views. Tucked away from the city, the home sits at the end of a private, gated street and is enveloped by explosive vistas from DTLA to the Pacific. The entry is itself a geometric statement piece with modern, cubic columns illuminated and framing the cityscape in the distance. With a sweeping open floor plan and endless indoor/outdoor flow, the compound is a true entertainer’s paradise.
