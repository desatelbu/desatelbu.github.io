Steven Blair presented his entertaining and informative talk on Physical Inactivity on October 23, 2013 at Creighton University in Omaha, Nebraska.
Steven N. Blair, P.E.D., FACSM, is Professor in the Departments of Exercise Science and Epidemiology and Biostatistics at the Arnold School of Public Health, University of South Carolina.
