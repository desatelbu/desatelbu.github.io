Our clients wanted a family garden that was not only beautiful to look at, but also reflected their cultural ancestry. Islamic gardens embrace a number of their key elements – clean lines, symmetry, sensory planting, the sound of running water and the use of polished paving and decorative screening and tiling – to name but a few!
