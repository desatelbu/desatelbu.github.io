I live in Barmouth on the west coast of Wales and create resin pictures. Started off with shoreline ones using stones/shells/sand and wood from Barmouth Beach, but have progressed into abstract ones. There are many different styles - it’s cool to experiment! But one thing that holds almost all of them together - they are supersparkly with lots of movement and colour changing. :D Sadly, the photos and videos really don’t do them justice. Find out more about my work, and Barmouth at https://barmouthbuys.co.uk
