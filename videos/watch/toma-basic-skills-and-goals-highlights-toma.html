Toma Basic Skills and Goals Highlights

Toma Bašić (Croatian Toma Bašić; November 25, 1996, Zagreb, Croatia) is a Croatian footballer, midfielder of the French club Bordeaux.

Basic is a graduate of "Hajduk", where he was admitted to the academy at the age of 11 and where he studied until 2014. After graduation, he went on loan for a year to the Croatian club "Rudesh", which was playing at that time in the second strongest Croatian league.

In the summer of 2015 he returned to "Hajduk", trained and went through the training camp together with the main team. On August 10, 2015 he made his debut in the Croatian championship in a duel against Lokomotiva, coming on as a substitute in the 84th minute instead of Andriy Balic. In total, in his debut season he played 4 matches and scored one goal. It happened on May 14, 2016 in a duel against Zagreb.

The player also kicked off the 2016/17 season with a shock, starting on July 17, 2016 in the starting lineup in the first round match against Tsibalia and scoring already on the 18th minute.
