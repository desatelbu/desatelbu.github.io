Khoresht-e-karafs is a wonderful and fragrant celery based stew with lamb. Like most Persian stews the key to its wonderful flavours is that it is slow cooked on low fire until all the flavours are infused together.

ingredients:
Lamb
Parsley 2 batches
Mint 1 batch
1 celery
Paste or orange juice
Salt, black pepper, red pepper
dried mint
An onion
Oil

00:00 start
01:29 Chop the celery and lamb
02:54 Chop the onions
03:59 Frying celery stalks
04:43 Mint, parsley, celery leaves
05:15 Washing rice
06:55 Add salt to the washed rice and let it soak for 1 hour
08:00 Chopping vegetables
09:13 Roast the vegetables a little, then add water with a glass and let it cook
11:07 Fry the onion and season it
12:15 Add the lamb to the hot onion and fry
13:30 Add a little dried mint
14:00 Add the fried vegetables and add a little water until the stew is completely absorbed
15:24 Adjust the water and salt of the rice, add a little oil and let it cook
15:55 We make Shirazi salad, a combination of cucumber, tomato, onion, dried mint, juice, and spices
17:30 making tea
18:55 Brewing saffron
19:35 Tea and sweets
22:20 Heat a little oil for saffron rice
22:36 Add orange paste and salt
23:37 Serving food
