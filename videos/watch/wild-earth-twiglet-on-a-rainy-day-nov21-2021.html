Twiglet is by himself on this rainy day ~ a sign that the owlet’s development is right on track. Mom and Dad will remain close and Mom will be returning to the nest in awhile. Twiglet may be looking wet but he is just fine-doing what all birds do-waiting for the rain to stop and hoping Mom returns with some food!
