In this episode of Off-Script, Sohla el-Waylly is here to show you how to make any kind of chicken soup you want. Learn her method to make a very simple broth for when you really need warmth and comfort. She shares her recipe for a gingery chicken soup with hearty lentils and potatoes as well as a spicy version with cabbage and sweet potatoes. Just what we need on those cold days.
