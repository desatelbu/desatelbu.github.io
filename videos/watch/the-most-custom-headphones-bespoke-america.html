Silicone goop, home fit-kits, and dual-camera wielding guns that fire lasers directly into your brain. These are just a few of the tools of the trade used in making the customest custom in-ear monitors imaginable. But it goes deeper than that.

From choosing how many drivers you want (up to eight tiny speakers per ear!), to customizing the way they look, and making sure they fit every tiny nook and cranny of your ear, the precision and craft that go into these little things is mind-boggling. Oh yeah, and they sound amazing, too.

Join host Brent Rose as he digs into the art and science of all things custom in Bespoke America.
