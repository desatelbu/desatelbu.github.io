The Humanity presents you "RIYADH: Saudi Arabia has allowed direct entry of travelers from Indonesia, Pakistan, Brazil, Vietnam, Egypt and India starting from next Wednesday corresponding to December 1st, 2021

Travelers will require to quarantine for 5 days at a government approved facility irrespective of their immunization status outside the Kingdom
