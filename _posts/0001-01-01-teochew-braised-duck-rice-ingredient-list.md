{% raw %}Teochew Braised Duck Rice<br />Ingredients A：<br />500g of duck breast<br />1 pork intestine<br />2 tau kwa (firm tofu)<br />4 hard boiled chicken eggs<br /><br />Ingredients B - Fresh Herbs:<br />2 fresh coriander’s roots<br />2 tablespoons of chopped shallot<br />Few slices of ginger<br />1 tablespoon of chopped garlic<br /><br />Ingredients C - Dried Herbs:<br />2 cinnamon stick<br />1 bay leaf<br />2 star anise<br />½ teaspoon of black peppercorn<br />4 cardamoms<br />3 cloves<br />1 black cardamom, cracked<br />1 teaspoon of five spice powder<br /><br />Ingredients D:<br />1 tablespoon of rock sugar<br />1 tablespoon of fried shallot<br /><br />Ingredients E (For Garnishing):<br />Chopped spring onion<br />Fried garlic<br /><br />Instruction For Blanching Process:<br />1) Wash and clean the duck breast.<br />2) Rinse well on the pork intestine and soak it under the water mixed with white vinegar for about 15 minutes.<br />3) Prepare a wok, add a few slices of ginger and some rice cooking wine into the wok.<br />4) Put the cleaned duck breast and pork intestine into the wok, immerse them with enough amount of room temperature water<br />5) Heat up the wok, use high heat to boil them for about 2 to 3 minutes.<br /><br />Stir-fry Process:<br />1) Using the La Gourmet pressure cooker to do the stir-fry.<br />1) Add some cooking oil into the pressure cooker, heat up the oil.<br />2) Put the Ingredients B into the pressure cooker and saute them till fragrant.<br />3) Put the Ingredients C and D into the pressure cooker, saute them till fragrant.<br />4) Add in at least 2L of hot water, make sure the water level is good enough to submerge all the ingredients.<br />5) Add in 3 to 4 tablespoons of light soy sauce.<br />6) Add in 4 to 5 tablespoons of dark soy sauce.<br />7) Add in 4 to 5 tablespoons of Shaoxing cooking wine.<br />8) Add in 1 tablespoon of fish sauce.<br />9) Add in ½ teaspoon of salt.<br /><br />Pressure Cooking Process:<br />1) Put in the Ingredients A into the pressure cooker.<br />2) Close and lock the lid of pressure cooker.<br />3) Press the cancel button of “open lid” cooking.<br />4) Select the “CHICKEN” button to pressure cook for 15 minutes.<br />5) Release the steam, open the lid of pressure cooker, and take out the braised pork intestine.<br />6) Close the lid, select the “STEW” button to continue pressure cook for 25 minutes.<br />7) Release the steam, open the lid of pressure cooker, and take out the braised duck, braised tau kwa and the braised eggs.<br />8) Prepare a serving plate and place some sliced cucumbers on the plate.<br />9) Cut the braised pork intestine, braised duck, braised tau kwa and the braised eggs into mouth bite size and put them on the serving plate.<br />10) Pour some braised broth on top and garnish with some chopped spring onion and fried garlic.<br />11) Serve with steamed rice, stir-fried cabbage, soup and chili sauce.<br /><br />Soup:<br />1) Prepare a pan and add in a bowl of chicken stock.<br />2) Season the chicken stock with a little bit of soy sauce.<br />3) Heat up the chicken stock.<br />4) Prepare a serving bowl aside.<br />5) Take out chicken stock from the pan and pour into the serving bowl.<br />6) Take out some braised broth from the pressure cooker and mix with the chicken stock.<br />7) Add in some
