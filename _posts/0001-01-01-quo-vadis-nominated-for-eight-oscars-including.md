{% raw %}Nominated for eight Oscars, including Best Picture, this is the powerful story of a Roman soldier whose romance with a beautiful woman puts him at deadly odds with Emperor Nero. Based on the classic novel by Henry Sienkiewicz, it features stupendous battle scenes. Starring Robert Taylor (&quot;Ivanhoe&quot;), and Oscar-winners Deborah Kerr (&quot;The King and I,&quot; &quot;From Here to Eternity&quot;) and Peter Ustinov (&quot;Spartacus&quot;), who was nominated along with Leo Genn (&quot;Henry V&quot;) for Best Supporting Actor. Directed by Oscar-winner director/producer Mervyn LeRoy (&quot;The Wizard of Oz,&quot; &quot;Little Caesar&quot;). Be on the lookout for cameos by screen icons Elizabeth Taylor and Sophia Loren.{% endraw %}
