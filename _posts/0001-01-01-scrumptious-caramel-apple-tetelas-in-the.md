{% raw %}Pastry chef Isabel Coss is back to share her recipe for scrumptious tetelas filled with spiced apples and queso fresco. Tetelas are traditionally a savory Mexican dish from pre-Colombian times. This version is sweet and is paired with a rich caramel sauce that’s perfect for dipping!
