{% raw %}0:00 - THE BIG STORY<br /><br />0:36 - ADVERSE EFFECTS OF MRNA BOOSTER SHOTS SIMILAR TO THOSE OF FIRST TWO DOSES: HSA<br />The adverse effects associated with mRNA Covid-19 booster shots are similar to those of the first two doses of such vaccines, and there is no increase in the frequency of such effects, the Health Sciences Authority (HSA) said in its Nov 16 report on the safety of Covid-19 vaccines.<br /><br />HSA said there were 200 adverse events reported after 854,268 people received the mRNA booster doses as at end-October. This amounted to 0.02 per cent of the doses administered.
