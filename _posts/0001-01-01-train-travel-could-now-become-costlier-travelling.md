{% raw %}Travelling by train could soon become a little more expensive as passengers will now have to pay an additional charge between Rs 10 and Rs 35 for boarding trains from stations that have been redeveloped or will be developed soon.  That have been redeveloped or will be developed soon.  Similar charges will also be charged from passengers at stations that have been redesigned or new stations. The charge will be added to the ticket.<br />There are five categories of this fee. The highest fee will be charged in AC first class followed by AC second, then passengers travelling in AC third and sleeper class.   The lowest fee will be charged from passengers travelling in the general category.<br />Sources said the decision in this regard was earlier sent to the Cabinet by the Indian Railways. .
