{% raw %}SCP 5962 is a Euclid Class anomaly also known as One Night at McDonalds.<br /><br />SCP-5962 is an obese humanoid resembling the McDonald's fast-food mascot known as &quot;Ronald McDonald&quot;.<br />Despite its humanoid appearance, SCP5962 is genetically identical to cows (Bos taurus). It lacks numerous internal organs, and instead have 27 interconnected stomachs of varying sizes that serve no biological function.<br />SCP-5962 will unhinge its jaw before producing packaged items from the McDonald's menu from out of its mouth.
