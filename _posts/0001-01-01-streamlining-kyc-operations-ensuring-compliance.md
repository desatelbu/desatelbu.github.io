{% raw %}Banking Technology Webinar with Bank Of Irelend and Dell Services:<br /><br />In the past 2 years alone, large global banks have been fined more than $3Bn in fines for breaches of AML and CDD. As a result, global regulators have heightened the scrutiny of AML programs. Compliance Officers are seeking enhanced functions and improved processes from their KYC programs.<br /><br />Presenters from Banking Technology, Dell Services and Bank of Ireland provide a bank’s perspective on how they solved the KYC Challenge.<br /><br />Some of their key challenges include:<br /><br />•Lack of a single view for customer KYC information across multiple lines of business (Retail, Corporate, Investment) <br />•Manual interventions for KYC processes and lack of automation <br />•Non coherent KYC procedures <br />•Absence of a risk based approach <br /><br />During the webcast we will discuss:<br /><br />•What's happening in the regulatory landscape in relation to AML, KYC and CDD/EDD. <br />•What are some of the newer KYC approaches and solutions we see in the market today <br />•What constitutes a comprehensive integrated end to end KYC integrated solution? <br />•What type of automation can be provided for example to assist in searching structured &amp; unstructured web sources <br />•What type of governance might be required across business and technology functions to ensure a comprehensive approach <br />•Does it make sense to centralise KYC operations onto one platform and to 'best shore' the delivery model? <br />•How do you ensure best customer experience during the KYC process{% endraw %}
